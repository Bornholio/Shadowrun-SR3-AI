## District Character

Belltown sits at the edge of corporate Seattle and the Barrens fringe. Security rating fluctuates by block — C on the main corridors, E in the back alleys, occasionally lower. The gap between corporate coverage and Barrens chaos is where things operate that can't survive in either zone cleanly.

3rd Street is the spine of the family's current Belltown presence. Most active threads run through it or adjacent.
