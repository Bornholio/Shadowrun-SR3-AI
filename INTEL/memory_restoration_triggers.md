## Standing Notes

- Insect spirit hatred: **pre-conscious** — characters act before they know why. Do not telegraph.
- Dragon recognition: professional, not emotional — *"I have done this before."*
- Two Harlequin arcs (2050 and 2057) may conflate during restoration — GM manages which memories belong to which event.
- Althain (Mt. St. Helens): complete restoration for all seven. **Never surface to players as a destination.**
- Power costs (−0.5PP Magic) apply at point of restoration, not at trigger.
- Lightbringer powers restore 2 per volcano/Ghost Dance site event — cumulative across events.
