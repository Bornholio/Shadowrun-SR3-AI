# 02 — Sperethiel Lexicon
## Chat Alignment Header
**Purpose:** Word lookup and translation support for Earthdawn/Shadowrun game sessions.
**Use this document for:** Looking up existing Sperethiel words and phrases by meaning or spelling.
**Load order:** Load AFTER document 01 (Grammar). For word construction rules, prefixes, suffixes, and conjugation — see document 01.
**Source legend:** No marker = ED1 canonical | * = ED3 | † = Shadowrun | ** = constructed from roots

---

## Abbreviations
adj. = adjective | adv. = adverb | conj. = conjunction | interj. = interjection
mod. = modifier | n. = noun | prep. = preposition | v. = verb
(for) = formal | (inf) = informal | (ins) = insulting
