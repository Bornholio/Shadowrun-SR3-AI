# SR3 CORE CONCEPTS — ATTRIBUTES

**Edition Lock: SR3 only.**

---

## PHYSICAL ATTRIBUTES

| Attribute | Governs |
|---|---|
| **Body (BOD)** | Damage resistance, endurance, healing, bioware tolerance |
| **Quickness (QUI)** | Reflexes, speed, coordination, balance |
| **Strength (STR)** | Muscle output; size/metatype dependent |

---

## MENTAL ATTRIBUTES

| Attribute | Governs |
|---|---|
| **Charisma (CHA)** | Personal aura, social reads, face skills |
| **Intelligence (INT)** | Perception, analysis, memory, adaptability |
| **Willpower (WIL)** | Resolve, drain resistance, staying power |

---

## SPECIAL ATTRIBUTES

| Attribute | Governs |
|---|---|
| **Essence** | Life force; reduced by cyberware/damage. Reaches 0 = death. |
| **Magic** | Magical ability = Essence (rounded down). 0 = mundane. |
| **Reaction** | Initiative and reflex actions. Formula: **(QUI + INT) / 2**, rounded down. |

---

## STAT FORMAT

`Base (Augmented)[Cyber]`

- **Base** = unaugmented natural rating
- **(Augmented)** = with bioware/magic enhancement (natural for test purposes)
- **[Cyber]** = cyberware augmentation (not natural; excluded from magic/astral tests)

**Unaugmented stats apply to:** spellcasting, conjuring, drain resistance, astral tests, magical skill tests
**Augmented stats apply to:** physical combat, movement, physical skills, proximity social tests

---

## STAT MAXIMUMS

- Racial maximum = Rating 6 ± racial modifier
- Stat ceiling = racial max × 1.5, rounded down
- Cyberware and magic can exceed racial max — only ceiling applies to augmented totals
- Physical adept power increases (Improved Physical Attribute) count as **natural**, not augmented

## ENCUMBRANCE & CARRYING

| Load | Threshold | Effect after (BOD) turns |
|---|---|---|
| STR × 5 kg | Normal carry | No effect |
| STR × 10 kg | Heavy | Light Wound (Stun); +1 Stun/turn until dropped or unconscious |
| STR × 15 kg | Fatiguing | Moderate Wound; no run; movement ½ |
| STR × 20 kg | Exhausting | Serious Wound; no run; movement ¼ |
| > STR × 20 kg | Crushing | Immediate collapse |

**Lifting (not carrying):** May add STR×d6 kg to the STR×20 max. Hold limit = BOD turns; +1 Stun/turn after.
