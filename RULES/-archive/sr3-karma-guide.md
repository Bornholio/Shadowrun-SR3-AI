# SR3 Karma Rules

## Core Karma Types

**Good Karma (GK)** — awarded at end of adventure (not necessarily each session). Used to improve Attributes, skills, and special resources over time.

**Karma Pool** — subset of GK used during sessions to save characters in tight situations. Tracked separately from GK available.

**Total Karma earned** — running total, never decreases. Drives Karma Pool conversion.

---

## Karma Pool Conversion

- **Elves:** Every 20th point of total GK earned → +1 Karma Pool
- **Humans:** Every 10th point of total GK earned → +1 Karma Pool
- Conversion tracks against *total earned*, not available. Spending GK on advancement does not reset the counter.
- Pre-campaign GK (before Session 1) excluded from conversion counter — treated as spending bank only.

---

## Public vs Secret GK (SR2 rule — maintained in this campaign)

**Public GK** — earned through visible, witnessed actions. Feeds Reputation.
- Community service (1 GK/week sustained) is always public
- Heroic or notable acts witnessed by others may be public
- GM decides at point of award

**Secret GK** — earned through shadowrun work and private actions. Does not feed Reputation.
- Most run work is secret by default
- Clever unwitnessed actions are secret

**Total GK earned = Public GK + Secret GK** — both feed Karma Pool conversion.

---

## Reputation (Malice Campaign)

**Reputation = Public GK earned ÷ 20** (round down)

**Social recognition TN = 20 − Reputation**

- At Reputation 0: TN 20 (effectively unknown)
- At Reputation 5: TN 15
- At Reputation 10: TN 10
- Good Reputation 2 edge: separate flat modifier, applied on top of Reputation TN

**Identity tracking:** Reputation is currently unified under the Malice identity. If a clean identity break occurs, Public GK can be split by identity retroactively — the per-session tracking supports this. Tag: **(Malice)** on all current Reputation calculations.

## Awarding Karma — SR3 Canonical Categories

### Group Awards (all surviving members, equal amounts)
| Category | Notes |
|---|---|
| Survival | All surviving members receive this |
| Mission success | Full success > partial > failure |
| Danger level | GM judgment — calibrate to actual threat |

Typical group award range: 1–3 per category. Single adventure total rarely exceeds 12 GK.

### Individual Awards
| Category | Amount | Criteria |
|---|---|---|
| Good roleplaying | 1–2 | Stayed in character (1), exceptional (2) |
| Guts | 1–2 | Brave/effective above the call (1), particularly heroic (2). Stupidly brave does not qualify. |
| Smarts | 1 | Clever strategy, puzzle solved, good scam, or knowing when to run |
| Motivation | 1 | Drove the story forward independently, started plotlines without GM prompting |
| Right place/right time | 1 | Vital skill, unexpected need, not pre-planned. Do NOT award for planned skill use or good dice rolls alone. |
| Surprise | 1 | Surprising and effective strategy that foils GM's plans |
| Humor and drama | 1 | Paralyzed the group with in-character laughter, or delivered exceptional in-character drama |

**Hard ceiling:** Individual award greater than 12 GK for a single adventure is highly unlikely.

**Key distinction:** Do not award individual Karma for competent execution of role. A decker running a clean host is just playing their character. A decker who improvises a solution under fire to something nobody planned for — that's Smarts or Right Place/Right Time.

---

## Evaluation Checklist — End of Adventure

1. Did all characters survive? → Group: Survival
2. Was the mission objective met? Full/partial/fail? → Group: Mission success
3. How dangerous was it actually? → Group: Danger
4. Who stayed in character? Stood out? → Individual: Roleplaying
5. Who did something brave above their role? → Individual: Guts
6. Who solved something cleverly? → Individual: Smarts
7. Who drove the story on their own? → Individual: Motivation
8. Who had a vital skill nobody expected to need? → Individual: Right place/right time
9. Who surprised the GM? → Individual: Surprise
10. Who made the table laugh or delivered a memorable moment? → Individual: Humor/Drama
11. Is each award Public or Secret? → Tag before recording

## Community Service (Malice Campaign)

All seven maintained 1 GK/week community service throughout career.
- Always Public GK
- Rate: 1 GK per week sustained
- Source of Good Reputation 2 edge (pre-campaign, now baked into edge)
- From Session 1 forward: track as Public GK in session log at the rate it accrues
