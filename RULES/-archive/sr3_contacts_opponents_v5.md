## FORMAT NOTES

Stat order: `B Q S I W C M E R PR`
`M` present only for Awakened characters — omitted otherwise
Augmented values shown as `base(aug)` where applicable
`INIT` calculated separately — not included in stat block
Dice Pools listed as given — Combat pool penalties noted where specified
Cyberware and Bioware in separate sections where both present
`E` suffixes: `A` = spirit (exists primarily in astral space); `Z` = dual-natured
