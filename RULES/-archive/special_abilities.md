## Cognition
*Metamagic — Adept or Mystic Adept only (SotA:2064)*
*Prerequisite: Must already possess the Centering technique (MitS p.72) and a Centering skill*

Cognition allows an adept to hone and expand their mental, perceptual, and intellectual faculties. It reflects enhanced ability to interact with and comprehend their environment, as well as increased mental resilience and social and intellectual adaptability.

**In game terms:**
- Allows the adept to Center for success or reduce modifiers on **Intelligence Tests**, including Perception and Learning Tests — Free Action
- Adept may use Centering to reduce modifiers when **defaulting to Intelligence** from Intelligence-linked skills
- Adds **+1 die** to all Knowledge and Language Skill Tests
- Decreases the **Karma cost to increase skills by 1** (minimum 1 GK per rating point)

> **Note:** Each initiation grade can extend Centering to an additional category of Knowledge skill in place of a new metamagic. Perceptive bonus of +1–2 dice to Assensing and related perception tests applies at GM discretion — mechanical detail is thin in SotA:2064.

## Adept Powers

### Multi-Tasking
**Cost:** 0.5 Power Points

Multi-Tasking grants the adept the ability to gather information from multiple senses simultaneously and to concentrate on two separate actions or thoughts at the same time.

- The adept may use **two Free Actions per Initiative Phase**
- Example: an adept can read data off a vid-screen and simultaneously listen to the commlink, providing full attention and focus to each

---

### Nimble Fingers
**Cost:** 0.5 Power Points

Nimble Fingers allows the adept to perform simple reflex and timing tricks more efficiently and effectively.

- **+1 die** to Stealth or Quickness Tests involving sleight-of-hand actions
- The following become **Free Actions** for the adept:
  - Insert Clip
  - Pick Up / Put Down Object
  - Remove Clip
  - Use Simple Object
