## Anchoring
*Both · MitS*

- Bind a spell to a trigger object or condition
- Setup: Force × 2 hours of ritual work; requires lodge or library
- Trigger condition: physical (touch, spoken word), astral (aura match), or timed
- Drain occurs at trigger time, not during binding — caster need not be present

## Attunement (Animal)
*Adept · SotA:2064*

Bond with a friendly, mundane, non-sentient animal. Sentient/semi-sentient creatures (naga, sasquatch, shapeshifters, etc.) cannot be attuned.

**Familiarization:** Animal Handling (5) Test, base time 2 weeks. Animal must not be coerced. Extra successes reduce duration. Failure wastes time; restart.

**Bonding ritual:** After rapport established. Karma cost = animal's Essence. Ritual form tailored to character's cultural background/belief system.

**Bonded animal gains Sense Link** (MitS p.109) — adept can experience the world through its senses and issue simple telepathic commands (Animal Handling test may be required if command endangers the animal).

**Range:** (Magic + Charisma + Willpower) × 10m. Contact lost beyond range. If animal suffers Moderate Wound or greater while link active: link breaks immediately, adept suffers Light Stun.

**Group attunement (GM option):** Homogenous small animals (rats, sparrows) may be attuned as a single animal. Sense Link usable with any one at a time.

**Larger/paranormal/cybered animals (GM option):** Increase Animal Handling TN and Karma cost. Rule of thumb: +1 per critter power or Essence point lost to implants. Karma cost for cybered critters based on original Essence.

**Limits:** Total attuned animals/items ≤ Initiate Grade. No bonus from more than one attuned animal/item per action. Attunement severed voluntarily (Karma lost) or if animal dies.

## Attunement (Item)
*Adept · SotA:2064*

Bond with a vehicle, weapon, or piece of gear. Attuned item feels like a physical extension of the adept's body.

**Familiarization:** Relevant B/R Skill (5) Test, base time 1 week. Alternatively, adept builds/forges/constructs the item from scratch.

**Bonding ritual:** Karma cost = item's Object Resistance (SR3 p.182).

**Effect:** −1 TN to any skill tests involving use, control, or handling of the item.

**No bonus when:** item is controlled through a cybernetic interface (VCR, datajack, smartlink, etc.). Does not aid Magical skills (Sorcery, Conjuring, Centering, Enchanting).

**Limits:** Total attuned animals/items ≤ Initiate Grade. No bonus from more than one attuned item/animal per action. Attunement severed voluntarily (Karma lost) or if item is destroyed.

*Note: Attunement (Animal) and Attunement (Item) are separate metamagics — must be learned twice if both are wanted.*

## Absorption
*Both (Sorcery required) · SotA:2063*

Absorb spells targeted at protected characters, converting mana energy into a temporary dice pool.

**Mechanics:** Allocate Sorcery and Spell Pool dice to absorbing with the same limitations as spell defense. Each absorption success on a Spell Resistance Test removes one attacker success normally and adds one die to a temporary **Absorption Pool**.

**Absorption Pool:** Acts as a second Spell Pool for all purposes. Maximum = Magic × 2. Initiate may release any portion as a Free Action (dissipates harmlessly).

**Concentration:** Maintaining the pool is like sustaining a spell — +2 modifier, no Exclusive actions. If the initiate takes damage, make a Sorcery Test vs. (pool dice + injury modifiers) to maintain; failure = pool lost.

**Duration:** Can hold absorbed energy for a number of Combat Turns = initiate grade. After that, suffer 1 box Stun (or Physical if pool dice > Magic) at the end of each subsequent turn the pool is maintained.

**Drain:** At the end of any Combat Phase in which a spell was absorbed, make a Drain Resistance Test: Power = current pool dice, Level M. If Power exceeds Magic, Drain is Physical.

**Area spells:** Only protects characters with allocated dice. Unprotected characters still affected. Exception: area elemental manipulations — if any target successfully absorbs, the entire spell is absorbed (no others affected).

## Centering
*Both · MitS*

Free Action — declare before base skill test. Do not center when silence is required.

| Mode | When | TN | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| A — Success | Astrally perceiving | Same as base skill | Complementary dice (halved) added to base test |
| B — Drain | Before drain roll | Drain TN | Complementary dice (halved) added to drain resistance |
| C — Penalties | Before base test | Base TN − Initiate Grade | Every 2 successes = −1 TN on base test |

Mode B most effective — pool allocation flexibility, significant drain reduction benefit.

## Channeling
*Both · TAL*

Channel a bound spirit's powers through the initiate's body. Common among Voodoo houngans, Aborigine koradji, and wujen.

**Activation:** Exclusive Complex Action. Bound spirit must be present in astral space and in contact with initiate's aura. Conjuring Test vs. TN (spirit Force × 2 − initiate grade). Success = channeling lasts 10 minutes × successes. Failure = nothing. All 1s = spirit immediately goes free.

**While channeling:** Spirit's astral form is drawn into initiate's body.
- Physical attributes increased by spirit's Force
- All spirit powers available at spirit's Force rating (except Engulf and Materialization)
- Great form spirit: also grants Immunity (Normal Weapons) at spirit's Force
- Initiate gains spirit's vulnerabilities (e.g., fire elemental = vulnerable to water)
- Initiate retains all normal abilities
- Channeled spirit cannot be attacked in astral combat or have control contested; can be banished — initiate adds Charisma dice to spirit's Force Tests against banishing
- Astrally projecting ends channeling immediately

**Ending channeling:** When duration expires or initiate voluntarily ends it — spirit departs to metaplanes, all remaining services concluded regardless of count. Initiate immediately resists Drain: (spirit Force)D Stun with Charisma. If spirit Force > initiate's Magic, Drain is Physical.

## Cleansing
*Both · MitS*

Clears temporary background count. Cause must first be removed. Cannot affect lasting long-term background counts (Great Pyramids, Auschwitz, etc.) — GM final say. Erases astral traces and signatures in the area — assense before cleansing if information is needed.

- Initiate can only affect background count ≤ their grade
- **Test:** Sorcery vs TN (background count × 2) — reduced by 1 per 2 successes
- **Drain:** [background count level]D resisted with Willpower
- **Time:** Complex Actions = background count level × 2
- Requires astral perception or projection

## Cognition
*Adept/Mystic Adept · SotA:2064*
*Prerequisite: Centering + a Centering skill*
*Possessed by: Singer, Banshee, Carpenter*

- Center for success or reduce modifiers on **Intelligence Tests** (Perception, Learning) — Free Action
- Center to reduce modifiers when **defaulting to INT** from INT-linked skills
- **+1 die** to all Knowledge and Language Skill Tests
- **−1 Karma cost** to increase skills (minimum 1 GK per rating point)
- Each initiation grade can extend Centering to an additional Knowledge skill category in place of a new metamagic

*Note: Perceptive bonus of +1–2 dice to Assensing and related tests at GM discretion — detail thin in SotA:2064.*

## Divining
*Both · MitS*

Gain insight into future events. Requires assensing the subject or a magical link (anything suitable as a ritual sorcery material link).

Requires two skills: **Divining Skill** (Active Magical, linked to Willpower — cannot exceed prediction skill rating) and a **prediction skill** (Active or Knowledge, one only).

**Common prediction skills:** Astrology (birth date + charts required), Dowsing (pendulum/map, yes/no answers), Omens (natural phenomena, outdoor/wilderness best), Dreaming (trance/sleep, symbolic answers), Sortilage (runes/I Ching/Tarot — each tool a separate skill).

**Test:** GM rolls Divining Skill dice vs TN:

| Question | TN |
|---|---|
| Very vague (*What does the next month hold?*) | 4 |
| Vague (*Are my old enemies catching up?*) | 5 |
| General (*Will I get hurt on this run?*) | 6 |
| Specific (*Will Mr. Johnson take a bribe?*) | 8 |
| Very specific (*Is he picking up the bribe tonight?*) | 10 |

**Results:** 0 suc = no useful answer; all 1s = misleading; 1–2 = cryptic truth; 3–4 = mildly helpful; 5+ = approximately the detail requested. GM adjusts specificity to suit story.

**Limits:** Tests per week = initiate grade. Repeated tests on same subject: +2 TN per attempt until situation changes significantly.

## Empower Animal Companion
*Adept · SotA:2064*
*Prerequisite: Attunement (Animal)*

Expand the bond with an attuned animal to share adept powers with it.

**Ritual:** Similar to original attunement ritual. Karma cost = chosen power's power point cost × 4.

**Activation:** Free Action. Transfers one chosen power to the animal via Sense Link. Adept cannot use a transferred power while the animal holds it. Adept and animal must be within Sense Link range.

**Skill/attribute tests for transferred powers:** Use animal's Reaction as default (Intelligence or Quickness may also be appropriate per GM).

**Limits:** Multiple powers may be ritualized for transfer. Multiple animal companions can be empowered. A single power cannot be transferred to more than one animal simultaneously. Total power points transferred at once ≤ Initiate Grade (some costly powers may be untransferable at lower grades).

## Filtering
*Both (Cleansing prerequisite) · SotA:2063*

Construct a temporary mana "filter" to reduce background count penalties for the initiate only. Works on both temporary and permanent background counts, aspected power sites, and mana warps. Does not cleanse — background count remains unchanged for all others.

**Activation:** Exclusive Complex Action. Magic Test vs. TN = background count. Every 2 successes reduce effective background count against the initiate by 1.

**Duration:** (initiate grade) × (10 − background count) Combat Turns, after which the filter clogs and must be reapplied.

## Geomancy
*Both · SotA:2063*

Deals with manalines (ley lines, song lines, dragon lines), power sites, and astral topography. Geomancers are particularly familiar with Sensing, Cleansing, and Divining metamagics. Additional detail: MitS pp.84–85, TAL pp.99–100.

### Imprinting

A magical group that controls a manaline or power site can imprint their own aspect to its mana.

**Temporary imprint:** Control the site for at least one lunar month (28 days) with regular ritual and mundane use.

**Sustained imprint:** Use the site regularly — minimum 8 times per year. Site remains aspected to that group for at least one year while use continues.

**Permanent imprint:** Minimum 5 years of unopposed use. Cleansing metamagic can combat permanency if the background count has not yet become permanent.

*Note: Geomancy is a broad tradition with significant campaign and GM latitude. Corporations (Wuxing) and secretive societies (Druids) actively suppress public knowledge of its mechanics.*

## Infusion
*Adept · SotA:2064*
*Prerequisite: Centering + a Centering skill*

Temporarily extend existing powers or manifest new ones at the cost of post-use burnout.

**Activation:** Exclusive Complex Action. Centering Skill (4) Test. Each success = 0.25 power points to boost existing or acquire new powers. Max successes = Initiate Grade. Only one level of a power the adept does not already possess may be purchased.

**Duration:** Magic × Initiate Grade minutes.

**After powers fade:** adept suffers Drain = (Centering successes), Stun, resisted by Willpower. Additionally, loses the same number of power points' worth of powers for a period equal to the minutes the temporary powers were active. GM chooses which powers burn out — adept does not know until trying to use them.

**Selection limit:** New power selection is limited by Initiate Grade in power points total. GM may track incrementally or ask player to designate available powers at each initiation. Once the limit is reached, only previously chosen powers can be temporarily gained.

## Invoking
*Mage · MitS*

Summon great form spirits. Only initiates who can conjure spirits may use this. No great form exists for watchers, allies, or ancestor spirits.

Declare intent and specify great form bonuses before the initial Conjuring Test. Summoning proceeds normally, then two additional instantaneous tests (no extra actions):

1. **Conjuring Test** vs Force × 2 − initiate grade — success = great form achieved; failure = spirit uncontrolled in normal form
2. **Drain Resistance Test** as if spirit Force = (Force × 2) − initiate grade. Normal Force determines whether Drain is Physical. Centering successes from the initial summoning apply — no new Centering test. If Drain kills/knocks out summoner, great form spirit becomes uncontrolled.

## Possessing
*Mage · MitS*

Astrally projecting initiate takes control of another being's body. Only astrally active beings (including dual critters) can be targeted.

**Taking control (unwilling):** Astral combat — Stun damage only. Win = enter and control body. Stun not applied to host until initiate leaves. Max duration: grade in hours.

**Willing hosts:** must be astrally active. Non-sentient = never willing. Unconscious dual beings cannot resist. Cannot possess: no-body beings, Materialization-power bodies, or an astrally projecting mage's body (not dual-natured while projecting). Must be at the body's physical location with its astral form.

**Special cases:**
- *Projecting mage:* can possess their body if beaten in astral combat and body is in line of sight — must do so immediately after defeating them
- *Riggers/deckers:* can be possessed if astrally active — immediately dumped from icon/vehicle on possession

**While possessing:** dual being. Uses host's Physical Attributes, own Mental Attributes. Access to host's physical and innate magical abilities (geasa still apply). No access to host's skills, spells, or memories. Drain is Physical on possessor's astral form. Unwilling/unconscious host: +2 TN to all actions. Physical damage goes to host; mana-based magic affects the possessor. Can mask host's aura if possessor knows Masking — treat host as focus at Force = Essence. Can pass through own barriers; cannot pass through host's barriers.

**Ejection/disruption:** host killed or knocked out; successfully banished (Opposed Magic Test); astral combat kills/disrupts possessor; duration (grade in hours) expires.

**On departure:** host immediately suffers all Stun from initial astral combat. Willing host must resist Drain: Power = possessor Magic + grade, Level Deadly, Stun damage, resisted with Willpower — cumulative with any damage taken during possession. Host remembers nothing.

## Psychometry
*Both (Astral Perception required) · SotA:2063*

Read psychic impressions left on objects, places, or persons. Requires physical contact with the subject and astral assensing.

**Test:** GM rolls initiate's Aura Reading skill vs. TN from table below. All 1s = vivid but completely false information.

**Duration of reading:** Base time = (final TN − initiate grade) minutes, minimum 1 min. Maintaining psychometry = Exclusive Complex Action; +4 to Perception Tests while active. Breaking off early requires Willpower (6) Test.

**Backlash:** After a successful reading, roll Willpower vs. TN (12 − modified TN used). Failure = Light Stun. All 1s = temporary phobia (Willpower 6 to use again); phobia lasts (10 − Willpower) days.

**Repeat attempts:** +2 TN per subsequent attempt on same subject. Must wait (10 − initiate grade) hours between attempts.

#### Psychometry Target Number

| Time Since Impressions Formed | Base TN |
|---|---:|
| Less than 1 day | 6 |
| Less than 1 month | 8 |
| Less than 1 year | 10 |
| 1 year or more | 12 |
| 10 years or more | 14 |
| Each additional 10 years | +2 |

#### TN Modifiers

| Condition | Modifier |
|---|---:|
| Subject is a place | +1 |
| Subject is a metal object (ring, watch, pendant) | +1 |
| Subject is a bonded focus | −(Force)\* |
| Strong connection between subject and impression's other parties | −2 |
| Initiate has strong connection to others in impression | −2 |
| Impression of violence or strong emotions | −2 |
| Impression of violent death | −3 |
| Chaotic/noisy surroundings | +1 |
| Subject carries more than one significant impression | +1 each |
| Astral signature(s) relating to impression erased | +1 |
| Cleansing metamagic used to remove impressions | +6 |
| Each subsequent attempt on same object | +2 |

\* Only for impressions related to the focus's bonder. Stacked foci give only the highest bonus.

#### Results

| Successes | Result |
|---|---|
| 0 | No information |
| 1 | Brief flashes, disjointed and superficial |
| 2 | Longer sequences, still disjointed |
| 3 | Greater detail, multiple senses, more coherent |
| 4+ | Lengthy coherent sequences, multiple senses, significant information |

## Quickening
*Mage · MitS*

- Make a sustained spell permanent — no ongoing concentration required
- Cost: 1 Karma per Force point, paid at time of quickening
- Still counts as sustained for dispelling and astral detection
- Can be dispelled normally; if dispelled, Karma is lost

## Reflecting
*Both · MitS*

Reflect spells back at the caster. Allocate Sorcery and Spell Pool dice with same limitations as Spell Defense. When a protected character is targeted, reflecting dice roll vs TN = spell Force and counter successes as Spell Defense. If reflecting successes exceed caster's successes, spell rebounds at caster at same Force — net successes become spellcasting dice for the reflected spell.

**Area spells:** only protects characters with allocated dice — others still affected. Reflected area spell hits caster only, not an area. Exception: area elemental manipulations — any successful reflect rebounds the entire spell and detonates around the caster.

**Counter-reflecting:** caster may use Spell Defense or Shielding against their own reflected spell. A reflected spell can be reflected again if the caster is also an initiate with Reflecting. Ritual sorcery: reflected spell affects all casting circle members equally.

## Sensing
*Both · TAL*

Detect fluctuations in the mana field and astral terrain without using astral perception.

**Detectable phenomena:** Background count, astral rifts, manalines, mana storms, mana surges, mana warps, alchera, astral shallows, and similar. Does NOT detect spells, wards, foci, spirits, or astrally projecting magicians.

**Range:** Grade × 500m base. Multiply by phenomenon's rating (if any) for maximum detection distance.

**Test:** Simple Action. TN 4, modified by anything affecting normal astral perception tests (not visual modifiers). Roll Intelligence + Grade dice.

| Successes | Information Gained |
|---|---|
| 0 | Nothing |
| 1–2 | General distance and direction of all detectable phenomena in range |
| 3–4 | Distance and direction, plus whether rating is weak (< Magic), moderate (= Magic), or strong (> Magic) |
| 5+ | All above, plus exact rating and general aspects/effects |

**Sustained sensing:** Counts as sustaining a spell; +2 TN to all actions. GM may make passive Sensing Tests (with optional +2 for lack of focus) when sudden astral shifts occur nearby.

## Severing
*Both (Astral Perception required) · SotA:2063*

Break a ritual sorcery link targeting self or another. Only usable after the ritual team has completed the Targeting stage and begun Sending. Initiate must know the link exists. Only works against ritual links — not spotters.

**Severing Test:** Magic vs. TN (ritual leader's Magic + 1 per additional ritual member). Must beat the ritual team's Linking Test successes.

| Condition | TN Modifier |
|---|---:|
| Per ritual member beyond the leader | +1 |
| Nature of ritual link unknown | +2 |
| Severing for someone/something other than self | +2 |

**Time:** (10 − initiate grade) × 10 minutes. Grade 10+ each grade above 9 reduces by 1 additional minute; minimum 1 minute. Severing fails if sending completes first or if the full time is not spent.

**Success:** Link broken; ritual ends immediately; ritual team must immediately make Drain Resistance Test. The material/symbolic link cannot be used for the same purpose for (severing initiate grade) hours.

**Failure (equal or fewer successes):** Ritual continues unhindered.

**Severing on behalf of others:** Initiate must maintain astral perception on the target for the full duration. Losing perception = severing fails.

**Focus links:** Severing does not break the focus bond. It temporarily negates the focus as a ritual link and makes it unusable to the bonder for (severing initiate grade) hours. The focus is not consumed; the bond does not need to be reestablished.

**Multiple attempts:** Each Severing Test is independent; successes are not cumulative. Count only the highest.

### Ritual Magic in Reverse

Using a person or object as a material link, a magician can target the tissue samples, hair, or other parts derived from that person — or target components once integral to a whole object — with ritual sorcery. Also applicable to reverse ritual tracking.

**Restrictions:** +2 TN modifier to Linking Test. Target material link must be specifically known. Only viable material links can be targeted.

Initiates with Symbolic Linking may apply it to reverse ritual magic, but the +2 modifier still applies along with Symbolic Links Table modifiers.

## Somatic Control
*Adept · SotA:2064*
*Prerequisite: Centering + a Centering skill*

Temporarily redistribute Physical attributes — raise one by lowering another.

**Activation:** Exclusive Complex Action. Centering Skill (4) Test. Max successes = Initiate Grade. Number of successes = max points that may be shifted between Physical attributes.

**Duration:** Magic × Initiate Grade minutes. After expiry, attributes revert and cannot be shifted again for 10 × the number of minutes they were modified.

**Skill penalty:** For every point an attribute is decreased, decrease all linked skills higher than the new value by 1.

**Drain on revert:** Body test vs Stun, Power = number of points shifted.

| Boost level | Damage Level |
|---|---|
| Above Racial Maximum | Serious |
| Above Racial Modified Limit | Moderate |
| Otherwise | Light |

Temporarily shifted attributes do not affect Karma cost of skills or learning. Changes to Quickness may affect Reaction, Initiative, and Combat Pool.

## Symbolic Linking
*Mage (Sorcery required) · SotA:2063*

Use symbolic links in place of material links for ritual sorcery Linking Tests, when the target cannot be seen physically or astrally and no material links are available.

**Link types and modifiers to Linking Test:**

| Link Type | Modifier |
|---|---:|
| Favored object (significant emotional value, frequently worn/carried) | per MitS Linking Table |
| Often-handled object (frequent use, some time in possession) | per MitS Linking Table |
| Recently handled object (5+ minutes in possession) | per MitS Linking Table |
| Symbolic representation (drawing, sculpture, doll) | per MitS Linking Table |
| Representation created by someone other than the user | +2 |

**Recently Handled Objects:** Usable for (target's Essence × 2) minutes. If the ritual begins before expiry, link holds for the ritual's duration. If ritual is interrupted, link is lost. An object that is Favored or Often-Handled for someone other than the target may cause the ritual to inadvertently target that other person instead (GM discretion).

**Symbolic Representations:** Creator makes an artistic Skill (4) Test with Representation Modifiers.

| Result | Effect |
|---|---|
| 3+ successes | Adequate link |
| 2 successes | +2 Linking Test modifier |
| 1 success | +4 Linking Test modifier |
| 0 successes | Unusable (creator may not know until Linking Test fails) |

#### Representation Modifiers

| Condition | Modifier |
|---|---:|
| Creator is familiar with the target | +0 |
| Creator has met the target | +2 |
| Creator has only seen the target | +4 |
| Creator last saw target more than 1 month ago | +6 |
| Creator has assensed the target | −2 |
| Target is in view during creation | −1 |

**Symbolic Link Foci:** A representation may be enchanted into a symbolic link focus using Enchanting (Artificing). Base formula time: 10 days. Enchanting TN: 4. Handmade telesma = −1 modifier. Force used as a modifier to the Linking Test. Max Force = 6 and cannot exceed successes on the artistic creation test.

**Reverse ritual magic with Symbolic Linking:** Initiates who know this metamagic may apply it to reverse ritual magic (see Severing entry), with the same +2 Linking Test modifier.

## Virtuoso
*Adept · SotA:2064*
*Prerequisite: Artist adept*

Pour talent and magic into an original artwork to create a singular, emotionally charged masterpiece. Both physical works and performance pieces qualify.

**Creation test:** Artistic Knowledge Skill (9) Test (Composition, Dancing, Drawing, Painting, Poetry, Sculpting, etc.), base time 1 month. Add Initiate Grade dice. Successes = piece rating (excess successes may reduce base time). Good Karma may boost rating at +1 per 2 Karma (Karma spent ≤ artistic skill rating).

**Effect:** Completed piece generates a Background Count = rating, aspected to the creator (does not interfere with them). Even mundanes find virtuoso pieces profoundly emotional. Applies Enthralling Performance (SotA:2064 p.65) to any character experiencing it for the first time (add Initiate Grade to Open Test).

**Performance art:** Composed as above. Each performance requires a separate Performance Skill (6) Test to evoke the emotional/Background Count effect. GM determines roleplaying side effects.

**Additional uses:**
- Physical pieces act as a material link (MitS p.34) to the creator
- Can serve as exotic materials for enchanting (piece is not destroyed; becomes integral to the final product)
