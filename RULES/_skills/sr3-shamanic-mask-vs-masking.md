# SR3 Reference — Shamanic Mask vs. Masking Metamagic
*Malice Family — SR3 Campaign*

---

## Two Separate Mechanics — No Overlap

These share a word and nothing else. One is physical. One is astral. They operate on different planes and stack independently.

---

## Shamanic Mask

**What it is:** A perceptual phenomenon. When a shaman uses magical skills, totem-associated features become apparent to physical observers — a kind of hallucination affecting those present.

**Plane:** Physical only. Mundane observers are affected. Does not appear on film or video.

**Scale:** Scales with power. Minor magic = subtle trait shifts. Demanding magic = full totem imagery overlaying the shaman's face and body.

**Detection:** Perception Test.

| Factor | Effect on TN |
|--------|-------------|
| Base | 4 |
| + Caster's Magic | + Magic |
| − Force of magic performed | − Force |

*Example: Magic 6, Force 4 → TN 6. Magic 6, Force 2 → TN 8.*

**Noticing Spellcasting Modifiers:**

| Situation | Modifier |
|-----------|----------|
| Observer is Awakened | −2 |
| Observer is astrally perceiving | −2 |
| Shamanic mask visible | −2 |
| Shaman using a Totem Advantage | −1 |
| Fetish-limited spell cast | −1 |

**Key point:** Cannot be suppressed. It is an involuntary visual manifestation of totem power.

---

## Masking (Initiate Metamagic)

**What it is:** An initiate ability that hides the true nature of an aura from astral observers — grade, initiacy, emotional state, dual-nature status.

**Plane:** Astral only. Mundane observers are completely unaffected.

**Default state:** An initiate's aura is always masked unless they deliberately reveal it.

**Detection — Standard (Secret GM Roll):**

Roll Magic dice vs. TN equal to target's Magic/Force/Essence.  
Successes needed = target's grade − viewer's grade (minimum 1).  
If target's grade ≤ viewer's grade: only 1 success required.

*Example: Grade 2 viewer (Magic 7) assensing Grade 4 target (Magic 6) → roll 7d6 vs TN 6, need 2 successes.*

**Failed attempt:** May try once more at +2 TN. If this also fails, the mask cannot be pierced.

**Deliberate Masking (Optional — Exclusive Simple Action):**

Roll (grade × 2) dice vs. TN equal to own Magic.  
Every 2 successes counter 1 success a viewer generates.  
Cannot perform any other magical activity while maintaining it. Drop as a Free Action.

**Foci and Spells:**

Masking covers the initiate plus Force Points of foci/sustained/quickened spells equal to grade, automatically. Beyond that, deliberate masking is required (TN = total Force in foci and spells).

**Masking an Astral Form:**

Dual-natured characters can mask their astral form to appear as a mere aura — concealing that they are astrally perceiving. The form still interacts with astral barriers and projecting magicians normally.  
Projecting characters cannot fully use this: no physical body visible = automatic tell to any observer who looks closely.

---

## Interaction

None. The two mechanics are independent.

A shaman casting a high-Force spell with a visible shamanic mask is simultaneously:
- **Physically obvious** — low TN Perception test, totem imagery blazing
- **Astrally unreadable** — any initiate below grade parity cannot assense their true aura

Suppressing the shamanic mask is not possible. Masking the aura is always on by default.
