## General

- Genetech modifications are **natural** — not augmented
- Multiple procedures simultaneously: costs and Bio Index cumulative, treatment time = longest single procedure only
- Genetic modification changes DNA, aura, and magical signature — previously taken ritual samples degrade: **+1 TN per 0.2 Bio Index gained, max +4**
- If genetech conflicts with cyberware or bioware, **genetech takes precedence**
- **In utero modifications:** half normal duration, 75% cost — does not apply to genengineered substances

## Gene Therapy

### Augmented Healing
- Heals all remaining damage boxes even if magical healing already applied; healing time halved
- Can restore damaged organs and regrow severed limbs
- With cyberware/bioware: +25% cost, same treatment time — cannot repair damaged cyber/bioware

### Cellular Repair
- Repairs otherwise irreparable somatic damage: permanent neurotoxin damage, Physical Flaws, attribute loss from disease or severe trauma
- Can reverse effects of boosted reflexes cyberware
- **Incompatible with:** boosted reflexes

### Age Rejuvenation
- Restores physical age to ~21
- Requires 1 major session + maintenance every 6 months for 3 years
- Base time: 1 month (age 40 or below) + 2 weeks per decade above 40
- Limited number of treatments before bodily systems fail
- With cyberware/bioware: +25% cost

### Phenotypic Alteration
- Cosmetic to major physical changes; any change not natural to metatype = major change
- Can increase **Racial Modified Limit** for Body, Strength, and/or Quickness by 1 each:
  - Separate treatment per attribute; each attribute once only; always major change
  - Also raises that attribute's **Maximum** to Racial Modified Limit × 1.5 (round up)
  - Does **not** increase current attribute score — limit/maximum only
- May be incompatible with organ replacements (cyberlimbs, cybereyes, orthoskin, etc.)

**Costs (gene therapy):**
- Major whole-body change: 2 months / 50,000¥
- Restore existing genetic pattern: 1 month / 30,000¥

## Environmental Microadaptation

### Cold Adaptation
- +2 dice to resist cold-related damage
- −2 TN to cold-environment Survival Tests
- **Incompatible with:** boosted reflexes, metabolic arrestor, heat microadaptation

### Heat Adaptation
- +2 dice to resist heat damage — applies to direct fire attacks (flamethrowers, fire spells, Flame Aura, ambient heat sources)
- Does **not** apply to ongoing burning damage (secondary escalating state: 6M base, +2 Power per Combat Turn)
- −2 TN to hot-environment Survival Tests
- **Incompatible with:** boosted reflexes, dermal sheathing/plating, orthoskin, suprathyroid gland, cold microadaptation

### Low-Oxygen Adaptation
- Holding-breath Athletics Test: each success = +10 Combat Turns (instead of +3)
- At normal oxygen levels: suffers hyperoxia — treat as oxygen toxicity

### Pollution Tolerance Adaptation
- +3 Body dice to resist chemical attacks from pollutants, heavy metals, and related toxins

## Direct Protein Synthesis

Enhancement reaches full effectiveness ~1 month after treatment. Significant physical effects may require longer treatment.

### Angiotensin
- After a Deadly physical wound: additional damage from blood loss and shock accrues at 1 box per (Body × 2) Combat Turns
- **Incompatible with:** muscle replacement, metabolic arrestor; negated if character has more than two cyberlimbs

### Calcitonin
- +1 Body for Damage Resistance Tests
- Bones do not break under normal circumstances; Barrier Rating 6
- **[HR]** Unarmed melee damage: (STR+2)M
- **Incompatible with:** muscle replacement, flex hands, platelet factory; negated if more than two cyberlimbs

### Erythropoietin
- +2 dice to all Athletics Tests
- **Incompatible with:** muscle replacement

### Glucagon
- Double all listed times when dealing with fatigue effects
- **Incompatible with:** suprathyroid gland

## DNA Masking

- Prevents automatic positive ID from DNA scanners
- **Opposed Test:** DNA masking rating vs DNA scanner rating
- Equal successes: scanner reports further identification required
- Scanner wins: full positive ID; excess successes may reduce scan time

**DNA scanner notes:**
- Scan results sufficient to hold a suspect — not legally admissible as forensic evidence
- Scan time vs masked target: scanner rating in minutes; reduced by excess successes if scanner wins
- No unregistered alterations: results in 1 minute

---

## Immunization

- Full immunity develops 1 month after treatment
- Complete resistance to normal doses of the specific compound
- Against excessive/abnormal exposure: immunity may fail, but Power of compound halved (round down)
- Similar compounds may be affected at GM discretion, possibly at half Power
- Max immunizations per character: Body (excluding race, cyber, bioware modifiers) — each permanent
- Does **not** work against neurotoxins or fast-acting compounds (Speed: Immediate) — use Cellular Repair instead
