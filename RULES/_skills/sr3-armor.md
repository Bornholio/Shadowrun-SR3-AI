# SR3 ARMOR — GM REFERENCE

**Edition Lock: SR3 only.**  
*Armor ratings table: see sr3-armor-types. DR TN calculation: see sr3-damage-conditions.*

---

## ARMOR TYPE vs ATTACK

| Attack Type | Armor Applied |
|---|---|
| Firearms / bullets | Ballistic |
| Projectile weapons, thrown | Impact |
| Explosives | Impact |
| Melee weapons | Impact |
| Stun ammunition | Impact |
| Damaging manipulation spells | Impact |
| Elemental manipulation spells | ½ Impact (round down) |

**Dermal armor** (cyber or natural): increases effective Body. Works against any attack. Does not aid healing.

---

## LAYERING

Three tiers in order. Formula applied separately to Ballistic and Impact.

| Tier | Examples |
|---|---|
| 1 — Form-fitting | Shirt, half-body, full-body suit — worn under clothing |
| 2 — Clothing-based | Vest, armor clothing, secure clothing — worn over form-fitting |
| 3 — Outer | Jacket or coat — worn over clothing-based |

**Formula:** Highest rated piece + ½ next highest (round down). Per rating independently.

**Stacking (not layering):** Helmet, riot shield, forearm guards — add ratings directly. Count toward QUI penalty. No formula applied.

**Restrictions:** Gel pack armor cannot layer. Hardened armor cannot layer with anything.

---

## COMBAT POOL PENALTY

Check Ballistic and Impact independently vs character's QUI.  
For every 2 full points either rating exceeds QUI → −1 Combat Pool die.  
Use whichever rating produces the larger penalty.

---

## MOVEMENT PENALTY (LAYERING)

Add Ballistic ratings of all worn pieces (including stacked).  
Each point total exceeds QUI = +1 TN on all QUI-linked tests + reduce QUI by same amount for movement.

---

## SPECIAL ARMOR TYPES

### Hardened Armor
Base Power (unmodified by burst/FA) ≤ armor rating → deflected, no damage.  
Base Power > rating → reduces Power normally.  
vs APDS / AV munitions: counts at half rating (round down).  
Needle/dart injection attacks: cannot penetrate.  
No other armor worn with hardened armor.

### Form-Fitting Body Armor
Layers under clothing-based armor normally.

### Gel Packs
Treat armor as hardened at normal B/I for deflection check.  
+2 to Knockdown Body Test.  
Combat Pool QUI penalty: treat B and I as 50% higher (round down).  
Cannot layer. Cannot add to form-fitting, diving, or hardened armor.

### Riot Shields
+2 TN to melee attacks for both sides.  
Reach +2 or greater bypasses shield entirely.  
Bash: (STR−2)L Stun.

---

## ELEMENTAL AND SPECIAL RESISTANCE

Modifications add directly to effective Power reduction for their damage type.

| Damage Type | Base Armor | Modification Adds To |
|---|---|---|
| Fire / heat | Impact | Fire Resistance rating |
| Cold / ice | Impact | Insulation rating |
| Electrical / shock | Impact | Nonconductive rating |
| Chemical (contact) | — | Chemical Seal rating reduces Power directly |
| Elemental manipulation | ½ Impact | Resistance rating adds to the halved value |

---

## BARRIERS

GM defines barrier type and rating. Reference table: sr3-armor-types.  
Firing through: Power reduced by target armor + Barrier Rating. Power < Barrier Rating → stopped (may still damage barrier).  
Regular door: opens when rating reduced to ½. Security door: must reach 0.
