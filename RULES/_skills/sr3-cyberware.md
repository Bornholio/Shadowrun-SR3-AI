# SR3 Cyberware
*Sources: SR3 core p. 297–303; Man & Machine. SR3 canonical.*

---

## GENERAL RULES

### Grades
`Basic` / `Alphaware` :: mass-produced; alphaware same availability as basic.
`Betaware` :: Availability +5/×1.5; not available to starting characters.
`Deltaware` :: Availability minimum +9/×3; corporate sponsorship required.
`Used (Thriftware)` :: 1D3 permanent Stress Points; cannot be removed. All grades except delta.

Essence Reduction: round all values up; never below 0.01.

Accessories to alpha/beta/delta must match grade. Components of a system (e.g., smartlink) must all be the same grade. Devices installed within a cyberlimb or body compartment do not need to match limb grade.

### Cyberware and Magic
Cyberware is integral to the body. Combat spells (Wreck, Ram), detection spells (Catalog, Analyze Device), and manipulation spells (Fix) cannot target cyberware in the body. Transformation manipulations (Shapechange) transform cyberware with the body — cyberware unusable in the new form. Removed from body: can be targeted normally. Can be enchanted before implantation only (MitS p. 39).

### Cyberware Triggers
`Activate/deactivate` :: Free Action (mental impulse).
`Manual trigger (subdermal switch)` :: Simple Action.

### Cyberware and Signature
Base Signature: 6 (5 for trolls).

| Essence | Signature Modifier |
|---|---|
| 0–3 | −1 |
| Cyberzombie | −1 per 2 points negative Essence |

### Increased Reflexes — Reflex Response
Characters with cybernetic reflex enhancements may react reflexively to threats: Perception Test (SR3 p. 231) at +1 TN per +1D6 Initiative bonus. Failure: instinctive reaction (GM). Particularly destructive action: Willpower vs unmodified Perception TN to avoid. Use sparingly.

### Interconnectivity
Datalink two pieces of cyberware through router or datajack. Standard datajack: 5 integral ports (1 neural, 1 external jackpoint, 3 dedicated internal). Devices on same datajack/router can exchange data. Accessories already integrally connected. Exception: image link substituting for smartlink eye display must be datajack/router linked.

Grade not a factor for linking. Data transfer: Simple Action regardless of size. Speed: 1,000 Mp/CT. Encrypted or compressed data: double Mp for transfer.

Mental access requires output device: display link (text), image link (images), knowsoft link (skillsofts), ear recorder (sound), simrig (simsense).

### Hacking Pool
Only encephalon and math SPU affect Hacking Pool. Both add their rating in dice; cumulative.

### Task Pool
Extra dice for skill use when activated. Uses standard dice pool rules (SR3 p. 43). Encephalon adds Task Pool only to Intelligence-linked skills. Does not affect Perception, Reaction calculation, or other dice pools.

### Vision Systems
`Low-light` :: Amplifies available light; useless in total darkness.
`Thermographic` :: Reads infrared. Effectiveness depends on temperature differential.
`Multiple systems` :: Can be used simultaneously; visibility modifier = lowest of any active system.

## DETECTION

### Security Cyberware Scanner
Hand-held Rating 3-6 / Doorway Rating 3-9 (Concealability 8). Roll device rating vs TN: basic cyberware TN 3, alphaware TN 6, betaware 8, deltaware 10. Catches cyberware and nano-implants. Does not catch bioware or free-floating/transient nanoware.

### Clinic/Hospital Implant Detection
Separate tests for cyberware and bioware. Each test: 60 minutes base. Rating = clinic/hospital rating. Visual scan first (Perception vs Concealability).

| Implant | Clinic TN | Street TN |
|---------|-----------|-----------|
| Cyberware Basic | 3 | 4 |
| Alphaware | 5 | 6 |
| Betaware | 7 | 8 |
| Deltaware | 9 | 10 |
| Bioware Basic | 5 | 6 |
| Cultured Bioware | 9 | 10 |
| Nanoware | 7 | 8 |

| Successes | Detail Revealed |
|-----------|----------------|
| 1 | Type of implant or nanite |
| 2 | Manufacturer, ratings |
| 3 | Grade |
| 4 | Stress or degradation amount |
| 5 | Options, nanite manufacturer |

No successes: reveals only base Essence Rating or Bio Index.

## HEADWARE — COMMUNICATIONS

### Commlink
Accessory for internal radio or phone. Scans/monitors Rating × 2 frequencies simultaneously. Programmable/lockable. Internal conference calls. Tridphone: voice-only unless image link present. Packaged with radio or phone: Essence halved.

### Radio
Transmit/receive any frequency; Rating × 2 programmed frequencies. Receiver-only models available. User subvocalizes when transmitting (inaudible to nearby persons).

### Subvocal Microphones
Allows communication without speaking aloud. Works with implanted radio or phone, or via datajack to external device.

### Telephone
Cellular network. Subject to ECM/jamming. Device Rating 2. Tridphone: voice-only unless image link. Fax receive; 5 Mp fax memory.

## HEADWARE — INTERNAL

### Chipjack
Specialized datajack for datasofts and knowsofts. Skillwire users can access activesofts. One chip per chipjack; multiple chipjacks permitted.

### Cranial Bombs
Illegal coercion devices. `Kink bomb` :: destroys headware or damages brain. `Microbomb` :: kills bearer only. `Area bomb (cranial nuke)` :: Power 3–8, Damage M–D, blast radius −1 Power/meter; can be placed in other body locations. Detonation: remote, timer, or sound recognition.

### Datajack
Standard I/O to cyberware and gear. Enables cybernetic interface with decks, RC decks, vehicles. Mental access to headware memory. Data output to display link, image link, or headware memory.

### Data Lock
Datajack encryption. Requires special code for I/O. Makes headware hacking more difficult. Cost: 1,000¥ + Data Encryption Cost (SR3 p. 291).

### Knowsoft Link
Mental access to knowsofts in headware memory or via datajack.

### Memory (Headware)
Storage in megapulses (Mp). Records from camera, datajack, ear recorder, opticam, simrig, etc. Output through datajack, comms, knowsoft link, display link, or image link.

### Tooth Compartment
`Breakable` :: Willpower (2) to break; Willpower (4) if lethal. Replace in 3 minutes. `Storage` :: Smuggles contraband; remove Quickness (4) + 2 Complex Actions.

## HEADWARE — BRAINWARE

### ASIST Converter
Converts simsense to/from metahuman brain-readable format. Built into simdecks, cyberdecks, RC decks. Available as external plug-in or integral datajack modification (no extra Essence).

### Chipjack Expert Driver
Task Pool = rating for skill on installed chip. Max rating 3. Purchased separately per chipjack.

### Cranial Cyberdeck
Standard cyberdeck rules. Active memory = implanted RAM (active utilities only). Headware memory usable as both active and storage. Other linked memory: storage only.

### Data Compactor
Compresses headware memory. −20% size per rating (Rating 3 = −60%). Character can exempt individual files. Integrated with data lock: −50% cost and Essence. Access times: Rating 1–2 Simple Action; Rating 3–4 Complex Action.

### Data Filter
Blocks short→long-term memory transfer while active. +2 TN all Perception Tests while active. Auto-deactivate timer available. Remote activation via radio (Flux 0). Does not block cyberware recording devices.

### Encephalon
Task Pool = rating for Intelligence-linked skills. Decking: adds rating to Hacking Pool (Task Pool does not apply while decking). Learning: add rating to Intelligence when calculating new skill costs. Does not boost magical ability.

### Induction Datajack
Requires magnetic induction adaptor. Functions as standard datajack. Invisible to visual scan; Concealability 10. Cyberscans: +1 TN to detect.

### IMS (Invoked Memory Stimulator)
Periodically triggers vivid memories. Primary use: cybermancy anchor. No specific game effects — GM roleplaying tool only.

### Math SPU
Adds twice its rating as Complementary Math Skill to math-related tests. Hacking Pool bonus = rating. Side functions: stopwatch, alarm clock, chronometer.

### Multi-Slot Chipjacks
Multiple chip slots; each acts as independent chipjack. Allows multiple softs simultaneously.

### RAS Override
Inhibits natural senses and muscle activation during simsense. +8 TN all Perception Tests and real-world actions while active. Some sims include built-in RAS disabler (simultaneous real/sim at up to +4 TN).

### Router
DNI junction device; up to 10 ports (not counting brain). Allows communication only — not cybernetic control. Non-headware connections require nanite-constructed microfilament fiberoptic cables.

### Tactical Computer
Expert system integrating multi-sense input for combat awareness. Background process; no actions required. Base: 5 dedicated ports. Additional ports purchasable. Rating = every 2 applicable senses (max rating 4).

| Rating | Combat Pool | SUT Bonus | Surprise |
|--------|-------------|-----------|---------|
| 1 | +1 | +1 | 25% |
| 2 | +2 | +2 | 50% |
| 3 | +3 | +3 | 75% |
| 4 | +4 | +4 | 100% |

No SUT skill: use bonus as skill rating. Does not assist rigging or decking. Sensory input via radio: each sense = 1 radio channel.

**BattleTac Master Unit (TC modified):** Transmits battlefield data to BattleTac receivers. Enables indirect fire, info sharing, SUT initiative bonuses. Simple Action to convey orders.

**BattleTac Cyberlink (separate implant):** Cybernetic BT receiver. Builds tactical map via image link or datajack. −2 TN SUT tests when master is targeting user. Installed with orientation system: −50% cost and Essence.

### BattleTac System
Minimum: master + receiver. Master builds tactical picture; receivers access data. Feeding data: Simple Action. Accessing data: Simple Action. Cyberlink/TC users access automatically.

Drone network: rigger with BattleTac FDDM can enable indirect fire for FDDM-equipped drones. Drones cannot use indirect fire without BattleTac FDDM.

## HEADWARE — COMMUNICATIONS (M&M)

### Internal Voice Mask
Pitch/timbre distortion; voice unrecognizable. Cannot imitate specific voice. `Infrasound modulator` :: inaudible to most metahumans; penetrates barriers better. `High Frequency modulator` :: inaudible to most metahumans. Both: Simple Action single word/phrase; Complex Action full sentence.

### Subdermal Speakers
Vibrates bones directly; cannot be overheard. Accommodates datajack-linked comms gear.

### Transducer
Translates nonvocalized mental impulses to words and vice versa. No visible/audible evidence. Via datajack/router with radio/phone: send/receive without speaking. Two users connected via datajack: communicate mind-to-mind. Can convert speech to text for display link.

## CYBEREARS

Modification or replacement (usually both ears). Replacement accepts up to 0.5 Essence additional adjustments without further Essence loss.

### Balance Augmenter
−2 TN balance tests (climbing, narrow platforms, landing). −2 TN Knockdown avoidance. Willpower (4) to intentionally fall.

### Dampener
Protects from sudden loud sounds and damaging frequencies.

### Hearing Amplification
Hears as if ten times closer to source. Dampener highly recommended.

### High/Low Frequency
Hearing above (high) or below (low) normal human range.

### Recorder
Outputs to headware memory, comm headware, or datajack.

### Select Sound Filter (Sound Discrimination)
+1 die/level listening Perception Tests. Opposed Tests vs white noise generators: double filter level. Max level 5.

### Spatial Recognizer
−2 TN sound-location Perception Tests. With high-frequency hearing: additional −1 TN. With orientation system: multiply all modifiers ×1.5.

## CYBEREYES

Replacement almost always involves both eyes. Up to 0.5 Essence additional enhancements without further Essence loss. Replacing natural eyes loses racial vision benefits (can be reinstalled). Max 1.2 Essence accessories per pair; max 1 laser, 1 opticam, or 1 weapon system per eye.

Single cybereye: 60% cost, 0.1 Essence, 0.25 Essence accessory capacity. Accessories requiring both eyes: ~half effectiveness in single eye (−25% cost and Essence). Vision magnification in single eye: +2 TN distance judgment while active.

### Camera
Digital still photos. Store in headware memory, datajack, or removable 1 Mp chip (~60 shots).

### Display Link
Text-only data from headware memory or datajack as field-of-vision window.

### Eye Datajack
Behind iris; Concealability 10 closed. Eye unusable while datajack active. Functions as standard datajack.

### Eye Laser Systems
Requires cybereyes. LOS only; no Flux Rating.

| Power | Effective Range | Blinding Bonus Dice | Battery Life |
|-------|----------------|---------------------|--------------|
| Low | 5 m | +1 | 100 min |
| Medium | 50 m | +2 | 50 min |
| High | 500 m | +3 | 25 min |

Battery cost: 150¥. Replacement: Biotech (6), base 10 minutes.

`Blinding attack` :: Target in range with eye contact. Complex Action. Opposed Quickness (user gains bonus dice). Each net success: +1 modifier all target actions (max +8). Flare compensation: −2. Fades −2/CT.

`Laser Designator (High only)` :: Launch Weapons (Spotter). Spotter must maintain contact until fire lands; +2 TN other tests while spotting.

`Laser Microphone (Medium or High)` :: Reads window vibrations to hear through glass.

`Optical Scanning Link (Optiscan)` :: Two-user laser comm. Requires LOS and mutual eye contact. Immune to jamming; disrupted by heavy smoke or +4 Perception modifier. Requires transducer to transmit; display link or transducer to receive. Cannot handle simsense, rigging, or decking. Concealability 10.

`Tool Laser (High only, low-power ranges)` :: Cuts 5 cm line through Barrier Rating ≤10/CT. Weapon: Complex Action, Laser Weapons, +1 TN/meter, 4L, Power −1 ≤2m, −2 ≤4m. Cannot penetrate reflective metals or silvered glass.

### Eye Light Systems
Tight polarized beam; invisible unless staring directly at user.

`Eye Light` :: Reduces darkness −4 (−6 with low-light). Range 100 m; −1/10 m beyond.

`Brightlight/Superflash` :: Functions as flash-pak (SR3 p. 283). Burns out after 3 uses; 150¥ replacement.

`Superflash (high-powered)` :: Visible 3 km LOS; affects all facing user. 12M Stun Damage Resistance. Power −1/meter; −2 flare compensation. Confined space: Blast rules (SR3 p. 119). Roll 2D6 after each use: 2–3 = cybereyes burnt out. Single-use; 10¥ replacement.

### Eye Weapons
One per eye. Skill: Eye Gun (Quickness-linked). No aim enhancers/accessories. Each shot: 1 Stress to cybereye. Reload: Biotech (6), base 10 CTs.

`Eye Dart` :: Narcoject/toxin at half Power (round up); range = hold-out ÷2. Post-fire: +2 TN vision tests for (4−Body) turns (min 1). Ammo: 4× normal.

`Eye Gun` :: Hold-out pistol; range ÷2; +1 recoil modifier. Post-fire: +2 TN vision tests for (6−Body) turns (min 2). Ammo: 5× normal.

### Flare Compensation
Eliminates vision modifiers for glare and blinding flashes. Also protects thermographic vision from heat flashes/IR glare.

### Image Link
Images and video from headware memory or datajack.

### Independent Cybereyes
Eyes outside normal sockets. Each additional set beyond first: +1 TN all target numbers. Ranged attacks using only independent eye: +4 TN. Distance judgment: +2 TN.

### Internal GPS
Position to within 2 meters. Functions as Nav-Dat GPS without map data. Typically paired with Orientation System.

### Low-Light Vision
Normal vision down to starlight. Total darkness: still blind.

### Microscopic Vision
Magnifies up to 1,000× within 15 cm. Fine manipulation of micro objects: −2 TN.

### Opticam
Records viewed images to headware memory or datajack. 1 min video = 1 Mp.

### Orientation System
Integrates GPS and mapsofts. 3D position, elevation, tracked targets, distances via image link or datajack. Can create maps from sensory input. Linked to TC: counts as 2 senses.

### Protective Covers
1 point Impact armor to eye area. One-way reflective covers available.

### Retinal Clock
Time/date stamp in field of vision. Stopwatch and countdown timers.

### Retinal Duplication
Duplication of another's retinal pattern — capital crime. Rarely fools scanners consistently.

### Thermographic Vision
Infrared operation. Heat patterns visible regardless of light level. Strong heat sources act as glare.

### Ultrasound Vision
Requires both eyes (emitter/receiver). Overlays topographic echo-image. Reduces visibility modifiers by half (round up). With high-frequency hearing: −20% cost and Essence.

Magic interactions: Indirect sight illusions (Invisibility): character appears as outline, +4 TN to target magically. Indirect sound illusions (Silence): cloaked characters absent from ultrasound image. White noise generators: add rating to all ultrasound-based action TNs. Susceptible to both mana-based and physical indirect illusion spells.

### Vision Magnification
Magnifies as imaging scope (SR3 p. 280). Electronic and optical versions (optical required for magicians with cybereyes). Optical in natural eyes: Concealability 9.

## BODYWARE (SR3 Core)

### Bone Lacing
Reinforcing lattice. Plastic: not metal-detector detectable. Aluminum/titanium: metal-detector detectable.

| Type | Body | Impact | Ballistic | Barrier | Unarmed |
|------|------|--------|-----------|---------|---------|
| Plastic | +1 | — | — | 6 | (STR+2)M |
| Aluminum | +1 | +1 | — | 8 | (STR+3)M |
| Titanium | +2 | +1 | +1 | 10 | (STR+4)M |
| Ceramic (M&M) | +1 | +2 | — | 8 | (STR+3)M |
| Kevlar (M&M) | +1 | — | +1 | 5 | no bonus |

One type only. Armor cumulative with worn armor. May choose Physical damage for unarmed: Power halved (round up).

### Boosted Reflexes
One-time electrochemical treatment. Permanent; cannot be removed. Recipient can never use wired reflexes or VCR.

### Dermal Plating
Hard plates bonded to skin. Body increased by armor rating; does not assist healing.

### Dermal Sheath (M&M)
Semi-synthetic self-regenerative skin. Body = rating + 1. Impact armor = rating ÷ 2 (round up). `Ruthenium variant` :: functions as ruthenium polymers; color change Simple Action; can display text/images from linked sources.

### Filtration Systems (SR3 Core)
Reduce Power of any toxin or gas by system rating (max rating 10).

`Tracheal filters` :: smoke and most gases.
`Kidney replacement` :: injected drugs, slap patches, most diseases. Blocks all patches except trauma.
`Oral filters` :: oral poisons including alcohol. Blocks all patches except trauma.

### Fingertip Compartment
Storage in last finger joint. Commonly used for monofilament whip.

### Handblade
Retractable blade, side of hand. Damage: (STR+3)L.

### Hand Razors
2.5 cm blades replacing fingernails. Standard: (STR)L. Compressed carbide: (STR+2)L. Retractable available.

### Muscle Replacement
Synthetic vat-grown muscles. Adds rating to Strength and Quickness (not Reaction). Max +4. *Incompatible with muscle augmentation bioware.*

### Reaction Enhancer
Superconducting spinal column replacement. Each: +1 Reaction. Max 6. Compatible with other Reaction/Initiative boosters.

### Reflex Trigger
Accessory for wired reflexes. Turns on/off: Simple Action. Must be installed simultaneously with wired reflexes; cannot retrofit. Off: +4 TN to detect wired reflexes with cyberscanner.

### Simrig
Records wet simsense. Baseline: 1 Mp/sec; full-X: 3 Mp/sec. Also works as implanted simdeck.

### Simlink
Simsense transceiver accessory to simrig. Flux Rating = Device Rating ÷ 4 (round down). Includes transmitter, receiver, and recorder.

### Skillwires
Neuro-muscular controllers for activesofts. Maximum total Mp and max skillsoft rating chosen at purchase. Chipjack and memory separate.

### Smartlink
Targeting feedback loop for smartguns. Standard: palm induction pad to smartgun.

### Spur
Narrow blade on bone; retractable. Three-blade variant on back of hand available. Damage: (STR)M.

### Vehicle Control Rig (VCR)
Neuro-enhancers and muscular signal transfer. Each level: +2 Reaction, +1D6 Initiative (rigging only). Allows defaulting to Reaction for any Vehicle Skill at +2 modifier instead of +4. VCR 2 = +4 Reaction, +2D6 Initiative while rigging (SR3 core p. 203).

### Voice Modulator
Options: Increased Volume; Tonal Shift (impressions, bird calls); Secondary Pattern (illegal; uploads second vocal pattern); Playback (exact reproduction from memory or datajack).

### Wired Reflexes
Neural boosters and adrenaline stimulators. Each level: +2 Reaction, +1D6 Initiative.

## BODYWARE (M&M)

### Auto-Injector
Dispenses drugs into bloodstream. Standard: 1 dose; expanded: up to 5 doses. Trigger chosen at purchase (timer, radio, datajack, blood chemistry, etc.). `Reusable` :: external refill port; can connect to IV. `One-shot` :: concealed from visual scan.

### Balance Tail
~1 meter weighted cybernetic tail. Expert-system balance processor; not consciously controlled. −2 TN all balance tests. With balance augmenter: cumulative to −3 (not −4).

### Biomonitor System
Three components; piecemeal or package installation.

`Biomonitor` :: Implanted chest. Heart rate, blood pressure, respiratory rate, temperature, blood sugar/alcohol, cell counts. Outputs to display link, datajacked medkits, or diagnosis processor.

`Diagnosis Processor` :: Expert-system Biotech Skill 5. Identifies health threats; suggests remedies; programmable alerts. Can link to auto-injectors, comms, air tanks.

`Subdermal Display` :: Lower arm. Displays text through skin; subdermal pressure buttons; flashing warning if health threatened. Concealability 10 inactive.

### Body Compartments
Hollowed or replaced body sections. DNI-equipped devices controlled mentally if datajack-connected. Concealability 9+ items only; one per compartment. Cannot be detected visually; Concealability 10 physical searches; +1 TN cyber-detection.

`Injector compartment` :: 1 dose; needle (STR+3)L, max Moderate, won't pierce hardened armor. Dose replacement: 2 minutes.
`Lockpicks` :: Key-and-tumbler locks; Lockpicking (Quickness); base 20 sec × lock rating; requires both hands.
`Lockpick Gun` :: Motorized expert system; Lockpicking = device rating (max 10).
`Synthetic Fingerprint` :: Opposed: print rating vs scanner rating; net scanner successes = detected; tie = rescan.

### Cyberfins
Retractable hand/foot spurs and webbing. Swimming: half walking/running speed. Attack: hand razors −1 Power. Incompatible with standard swim fins or gloves.

### Cyberskull
Plasteel skull framework. Protects headware: −2 Stress per damage event to headware. Called shots to head: −1 Damage Level. Obvious: +4 TN Charisma/Social Skill Tests; +3 bonus Intimidation Open Tests.

### Cybertorso
Reinforced chest/abdomen. +1 Body. Protects internal bodyware as cyberskull protects headware. Reduces by half (round up) the additional Essence cost of cyberlimb Strength/Quickness enhancements beyond 3 points.

### Direct Neural Interface (DNI)
Links brain to DNI-adapted equipment. Output requires display link, image link, or other translation gear. Dedicated datajack connection; only DNI-adapted devices can be controlled through it.

### Flex Hands
Memory plastic hand bones. Deforms under continuous pressure; returns to shape. −2 TN Escape Artist and hand contortion. *Incompatible with bone lacing.* Toggled on/off.

### Foot Anchor
Retractable spur in heel. Cannot penetrate Barrier Rating >10. Each anchor: +50 kg hold (−5 kg/BR below 10). As weapon: (STR−1)M; movement halved; +1 TN Dodge. Anchored: +1 Recoil Reduction/foot; −1 TN Knockdown resistance/anchor.

### Hydraulic Jacks
Obvious leg modifications. Max rating 5. Jump distance: +rating × 20%. Athletics (Running): +rating dice. Athletics (Jumping): +rating dice. Fall absorption landing on feet: −rating Power. Landing on feet: Quickness (5); +1 TN/4 m beyond 5 m.

### Internal Air Tank
Below one lung (partial lung removal). Immune to airborne toxins while using tank. Capacity: ~75 minutes. Refill via intake valve: 5 minutes. Underwater: −1 TN treading water/floating. *Incompatible with extended volume bioware.*

Breach: roll 1D6 on 1, explodes — 10S to owner (worn armor and dermal plating do not protect; bone lacing and internal impact armor do). Nearby: Power −2/meter; subtract external impact armor.

### Jolt Alert
Electric pulse wake-up. Must link to triggering mechanism. Cannot rouse Deadly Stun victims.

### Magnetic System
Electromagnets in a limb. One per limb. +4 effective Strength holding/clinging metallic items; ~25 kg independently. Does not interfere with electronics.

### Move-By-Wire System
CNS in controlled seizure state. Incompatible with all other Reaction/Initiative-enhancing cyber or bioware. Cannot toggle on/off; no reflex trigger. Quickness bonus does not count when calculating Reaction.

| Rating | Quickness | Reaction | Initiative | Athletics/Stealth |
|--------|-----------|----------|------------|-------------------|
| 1 | +1 | +2 | +1D6 | +1 |
| 2 | +2 | +4 | +2D6 | +2 |
| 3 | +3 | +6 | +3D6 | +3 |
| 4 | +4 | +8 | +4D6 | +4 |

Rating 3: extra mandatory action end of 1st Initiative Pass/CT. Rating 4: end of 1st and 2nd Initiative Passes. Extra actions can be held; lost at start of next Combat Phase.

`Automatic Stress` :: Applies to Quickness and Reaction; removable by therapeutic surgery only.

| Rating | Stress Rate |
|--------|------------|
| 1 | 1 point per 6 months |
| 2 | 1 point per 4 months |
| 3 | 1 point per 2 months |
| 4 | 1 point per month |

Each Stress: Willpower (rating × 2) or develop TLE–x (−1 Charisma/related skills, +2 TN Perception in crucial circumstances, −2 Initiative, −1D6 Reaction).

Brain surgery for TLE–x: base TN 8; +2 TN second surgery; after two surgeries, permanent. Acute seizure (TLE–x + Moderate or worse wound in combat): Willpower (6) or 6M Stun + (1D6 × 10 turns seizure) + (1D6 × 10 minutes unconscious).

`CCSS (Catastrophic Clonic Seizure Syndrome)` :: Willpower (rating × 2) if move-by-wire fails due to Stress or loses Quickness/Reaction to Stress. Failure: voluntary action ceases; system permanently dysfunctional; potential long-term damage.

### OXSYS Cybergill
Both sides of neck. Extracts oxygen from water via osmosis. Immune to oxygen toxicity; vulnerable to nitrogen narcosis and decompression sickness. Indefinite underwater; ~1,000 hours use before replacement. Filter replacement: ~1,500¥.

### Retractable Climbing Claws
Fingertip retractable. −2 TN Climbing or Clinging Tests. Attack: (STR−1)L.

### Smartlink-2
Called Shot modifier: +2 (vs standard +4). Underslung weapons: −1 modifier. Works only with smartlink-2-equipped weapons. Missile launchers with inherent Intelligence Rating: cannot use any smartlink.

Required components: I/O device (palm induction pad), eye display, ballistics processor, limited simsense rig. Substitutions allowed. If any component non-implant: only −1 bonus.

`Rangefinder (SL2 only)` :: −1 TN long range; −2 TN extreme range. Not cumulative with image magnification range modifiers. With smartlinked grenade launcher + air-timed mini-grenades: scatter 3D6 → 1D6.

`PSS (Personalized Smartlink Safety)` :: Safety engaged unless in contact with authorized smartlink. Encryption Rating 8.

### Stepped Reflex Trigger Modification
Allows wired reflexes to operate at lower level than purchased. Simple Action to turn on/off or switch level (not above purchased rating).

## RIGGERWARE

### BattleTac FDDM Master Unit
Fire Direction Data Manager. One drone can spot for others via RC network; enables indirect fire. Requires master unit on RC deck; both drones must be FDDM-adapted.

### BattleTac IVIS Master Unit
Inter-Vehicle Information System. Complex group mission: Small Unit Tactics (Vehicle Tactics) TN 5; successes split between Comprehension Test extra dice and shared IVIS Pool. Pool lasts until group completes task or receives new orders. Not available to drones rigger has jumped into.

### Cranial Remote Deck (CRD)
Standard RC deck rules. Small size limits to Flux Rating 0. Connect to external or cyberlimb signal booster to increase range. Linked headware memory usable as RC storage memory.

### Remote Control ECCM
Protects CRD network from jamming and interference including solar activity.

### Remote Control Encryption Module (RCEM)
Encodes/decodes RC signal transmissions. Rating 1–10. Incompatible with standard broadcast encryption. Only Rigger Decryption Modules can break RCEM encryption.

### Rigger Decryption Module
Decrypts RC signals encoded by RCEM and encrypted CCSS security systems. Does not decrypt standard broadcast or data encryption. Rating 1–10.

### Rigger Protocol Emulation Module
Emulates protocols on contemporary rigged security systems and drones. Required for infiltrating/hijacking rigged security. Rating 1–10.

## CYBERWEAPONS

DNI required for reflexive fire. Without DNI: must trigger manually. Smartgun link requires weapon to be smartlink-equipped and connected via router to a smartlink system.

### Cyber Dartgun
Modeled on narcoject series. Treat as light pistol. One dose capacity.

### Cyberguns
Designed for cyberarms; may be implanted in natural limbs. Forearm mount, fires through palm port. Internal magazine. Concealability applies inside natural arm; obvious when firing or using external clip.

Permitted accessories: smartlink, laser sight, silencer (light/heavy pistol), sound suppresser (machine pistol/SMG). No recoil compensation.

### Cybersquirt
Two reservoirs: DMSO gel and chemical of choice. Forearm-mounted, palm spray nozzle. Chemical capacity: 10 rounds; gel capacity: 10 shots. CO2 canister: replace/recharge every 50 shots, 50¥. DMSO armor interaction: porous armor (clothing, Kevlar) offers no protection — reduce Power by half target's Impact Armor Rating (round up). Only fully sealed rigid armor or chem/bio suits: immunity. Stress leak: roll 1D6 each Stress — on 1 or 6, reservoirs leak onto owner.

### Cyber Taser
Cyberarm/hand; fires darts on 15 m wires to palm capacitor. Damage: 10S. 2 shots/reload. Battery: 10 uses before recharge. Standard taser range.

Reservoir accessory to cyber-implant blade or dart weapon. Coats blade/dart. 2-dose units; 1 dose/hit consumed. Refill: Biotech (6), base 10 minutes.

## SMALL UNIT TACTICS SKILL

Active Skill; Intelligence-linked. Specializations: BattleTac Systems, Vehicle Tactics, Matrix Tactics.

Convey analysis/orders on last action of a CT. Communicating to team: Complex Action. Own benefit only: no Complex Action required.

One SUT Test; individual team members may have different TNs. Maximum recipients = SUT skill rating (not including user).

Every 2 successes against a team member's TN: +1 Initiative next CT (cannot exceed recipient's Reaction).

Multiple characters boosting same member: only highest bonus applies, −1 for conflicting orders.

For ambush preparation: add dice (1 per 2 successes) to Reaction/Surprise Tests.

With BattleTac master component: Simple Action to convey orders; may also receive bonus to SUT skill.

Base rules: SR3 p. 93. Large quantities of obvious cyberware cause social disadvantages.
