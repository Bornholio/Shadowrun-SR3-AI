# SR3 CONJURING

*Source :: SR3 core rulebook*

---

## CORE MECHANICS

`Conjuring skill` :: used for Summoning, Controlling, and Banishing
`Force cap` :: cannot summon a spirit with Force > (Magic × 2)
`Tradition` :: shamans summon nature spirits only; mages summon elementals only; either may banish either type

---

## NATURE SPIRITS

`Who` :: shamans only
`Domain` :: shaman must be within the spirit's domain to summon it; spirit cannot leave its domain or extend powers beyond it
`Multiple domains` :: shaman chooses one domain at a time; shifting to another domain cancels remaining services from prior spirit
`Limit` :: one nature spirit per domain at a time

**Summoning:**
`Action` :: Exclusive Complex Action
`Test` :: Conjuring vs. TN equal to Force; totem modifiers and spirit foci dice apply; dice may be held for Drain
`Centering` :: Mode A (astrally perceiving — complementary dice halved, added to Conjuring Test) · Mode C (reduce TN penalties — every 2 successes = −1 TN)
`Result` :: successes = services owed; 0 successes = no spirit
`Drain` :: always required (see Conjuring Drain below)
`Duration` :: vanish at every sunrise/sunset regardless; remaining services are lost

**Stand-by:** After summoning, a shaman can place a nature spirit on stand-by within the domain. Calling a stand-by spirit is a Simple Action (not Exclusive).

**Leaving the domain:** Cancels all unused services. Any service already requested is still fulfilled. Spirit remains until that service is done or next sunrise/sunset, whichever comes first.

**Services:** Each use of a power = one service. Continual use of the same power on the same target(s) = one service. If parameters change (e.g., Concealment now covers more characters), another service is used. Attacking any number of foes = one service. Physical tasks in physical form = one service.

A nature spirit in astral form can only use mana powers or powers affecting its summoner via the magical link. Physical powers require physical form.

## ELEMENTALS

`Who` :: mages only
`Elements` :: Earth, Air, Fire, Water
`Binding limit` :: mage can bind a number of elementals equal to Charisma at one time

**Preparation:**
`Required` :: Conjuring library + hermetic circle of the correct type, both rated ≥ Force
`Materials` :: ~1,000¥ × Force from a talismonger (consumed on attempt)

| Elemental | Source Required |
|---|---|
| Fire | Bonfire, fireplace, or large brazier |
| Water | Large pool/tub, or circle near water |
| Air | Large quantities of burning incense |
| Earth | Man-sized heap of earth/clay/rock, or circle on open ground |

**Summoning:**
`Action` :: Exclusive Action; takes hours equal to Force
`Test` :: Conjuring vs. TN equal to Force; spirit foci dice apply; dice may be held for Drain
`Centering` :: Mode A (astrally perceiving — complementary dice halved, added to Conjuring Test) · Mode C (reduce TN penalties — every 2 successes = −1 TN)
`Interruption` :: ritual fails automatically; Drain still required
`Result` :: successes = services owed; 0 successes = no elemental; materials consumed either way
`Drain` :: always required; Charisma dice vs. TN equal to Force (see below)

**If Drain kills or knocks out the summoner:** elemental becomes uncontrolled. Elemental makes a Force (6) Test — success: flees from both planes; failure: attacks summoner (or nearest living being if summoner is dead), does not stop until killed, banished, or controlled.

**Calling a bound elemental:**
`Action` :: Exclusive Complex Action; multiple elementals of the same element may be called in one action
`Command` :: Simple Action once called
`Preferred form` :: astral; only assumes physical form when ordered
`24-hour rule` :: if a called elemental is present (astral or physical) for 24 hours, one additional service is consumed; does not apply while awaiting call

`Radius of control` :: (Willpower + Charisma + Magic) × 10 meters. Elemental will not leave this voluntarily unless given Remote Service or placed on stand-by. If forced outside radius, remaining services are canceled; last command is still fulfilled.

Summoner may order an elemental to obey another character as master (costs 1 service). Mundanes can use all services except spellcasting.

**Releasing a bound elemental:** Mage releases it freely; elemental departs.

## ELEMENTAL SERVICES

Elementals perform one service at a time. Each service costs 1 of the spirit's owed services to initiate.

**Aid Sorcery** — Extra dice for Sorcery Tests in one category, like an auxiliary Spell Pool. Each die provided reduces the elemental's Force by 1. Applies to any test in that category including Spell Defense and Dispelling. Only one elemental may Aid Sorcery per character at a time. Can be provided in astral form. When Force reaches 0, spirit vanishes but can be re-called (Exclusive Complex Action) at full Force.

| Elemental | Spell Category |
|---|---|
| Fire | Combat |
| Air | Detection |
| Water | Illusion |
| Earth | Manipulation |
| (none) | Health |

**Aid Study** — Adds Force in dice to a magician's attempt to learn one spell in the appropriate category. One spirit, one time per spell. Can be provided in astral form.

**Physical Service** — Elemental materializes and uses powers or fights. Any number of foes = one service.

**Spell Sustaining** — Elemental sustains a spell in its category. Lasts 1 Combat Turn per Force; loses 1 Force per turn. Mage may take over before Force reaches 0 to prevent spell ending. When Force reaches 0, elemental vanishes but can be re-called identically to Aid Sorcery. Can be provided in astral form.

Alternatively, the mage can bind an elemental permanently to a spell: sustains it for a number of days equal to Force, losing 1 Force permanently per day or part thereof. Cannot be recalled after this. Mage can release it early to end the spell, but it still departs. If killed or banished while sustaining, the spell ends.

**Remote Service** — Spirit is sent beyond its radius of control. Forfeits all other remaining services. Spirit pursues its task single-mindedly — even the summoner cannot halt it. Still bound, still counts toward Charisma limit. When complete, elemental is freed and vanishes. Can switch between astral and physical form as needed.

## CONJURING DRAIN

`Test` :: Charisma dice (adjusted by totem modifiers and spirit foci) vs. TN equal to Force; dice held from Conjuring Test may be added
`Centering` :: Mode B valid before drain roll — complementary dice (halved) added to drain resistance
`Physical damage` :: if spirit's Force > summoner's Magic Attribute

| Spirit's Force | Drain Level |
|---|---|
| ½ Charisma or less | L Stun |
| Charisma or less | M Stun |
| Greater than Charisma | S Stun |
| Greater than 1.5 × Charisma | D Stun |

## SPIRIT FORMS

Changing form :: Simple Action for the spirit

**Astral form:** All astral Attributes = Force. Can only use mana powers or powers affecting summoner via magical link. Immune to physical weapons; only mana spells affect it. Weapon foci can harm it but wielder must be on the astral plane. Can communicate with summoner; visible to astral space.

**Physical form:** Uses Materialization power. Functions as dual being. Physical Attributes per spirit type (see Spirits & Dragons, p. 260). Has Immunity to Normal Weapons: Armor = Force × 2 vs. all attacks.

**Force of personality attack:** A character in melee with a physical spirit may use Willpower instead of a combat skill (no Combat Pool). Weapon has no effect on base damage but Reach bonus applies. On success: (Charisma)M Stun. Spirit resists with Force only — no Combat Pool, no armor.

## SPIRIT COMBAT

Spirits use standard combat rules. Powers determine attack dice, TN, and whether Damage or Spell Resistance Tests apply.

| Form | Base Reaction | Initiative Dice | Modifier |
|---|---|---|---|
| Astral | Force | 1D6 | +20 |
| Physical | By spirit type | 1D6 | +10 |

Switching from astral to physical mid-Combat Turn: ineligible for another action for 2 Initiative Passes. Switching physical to astral: no modifier unless in that form at the start of the next Combat Turn.

Spirits receive new commands only on their master's actions. They Delay if they have none.

`Karma Pool` :: successes from Conjuring Test + 1
Spirits also have Combat Pool and Astral Combat Pool.

If a spirit is killed or disrupted, its summoner knows immediately.

**Elemental vulnerabilities:** Attacks from the opposing element increase Damage Level by 1, including in astral combat.

| Opposed Pairs |
|---|
| Fire vs. Water |
| Earth vs. Air |

**Nature spirit conflicts:** Higher-Force spirit wins. Power takes effect at a Force equal to the difference. Ties = no effect. Standard damage rules apply to damage-causing powers.

## CONTROLLING

`Who` :: shamans may contest nature spirits; mages may contest elementals
`Action` :: Exclusive Complex Action (challenger); controller may always resist regardless of available actions or physical presence

Both make a Conjuring Test vs. TN equal to the spirit's Force. Controller adds Charisma dice. Totem modifiers and spirit foci apply.

| Result | Outcome |
|---|---|
| Controller wins | No change; challenger makes Drain as if summoning |
| Challenger wins | Challenger gains control; 1 service per net success; both make Drain |
| Neither rolls successes | Both make Drain; elemental becomes uncontrolled / nature spirit vanishes |

**Uncontrolled elementals:** Make a test as if banishing (see below). On success, elemental is bound with 1 service. Additional services: Conjuring Test vs. Force; spirit foci apply; every 2 successes = 1 additional service.

---

## BANISHING

`Who` :: either tradition; either spirit type
`Action` :: Exclusive Complex Action

Banisher makes a Conjuring Test vs. TN equal to Force. If banisher is also the summoner, add Charisma dice. Spirit foci and totem modifiers apply.

Spirit makes a Force Test vs. TN equal to the banisher's Magic Attribute.

| Result | Outcome |
|---|---|
| Spirit wins | Banisher's Magic Attribute –1 per extra success (temporary) |
| Banisher wins | Spirit's Force –1 per net success |
| Tie | No change; contest continues |

Winner decides whether to continue. If continuing, neither can act until the winner's next Combat Phase.

`Spirit Force reaches 0` :: spirit is destroyed
`Banisher's Magic Attribute reaches 0` :: Deadly Stun damage; unconscious; spirit is free; immediately check for permanent Magic Loss

Temporarily reduced Magic Attribute or Force recovers at 1 point per hour.

---

## ASTRAL CONJURING

All Conjuring actions are Exclusive and cannot be performed while astrally projecting. A summoner may summon a spirit first, then astrally project and command it from the astral plane.
