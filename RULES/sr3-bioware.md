# SR3 Bioware
*Source: Man & Machine. SR3 canonical.*

---

## GENERAL RULES

### Grades
`Basic` :: generic organ donor stock. `Cultured` :: cloned from host's cellular matrix; beta-level or higher clinics only; not recommended for starting characters.

**Acquiring Basic as Cultured:**

| Cost | Bio Index | Availability |
|------|-----------|--------------|
| ×4 | ×0.75 | +2/×5 |

**Used Bioware:** 60% cost, 1 permanent Stress Point (cannot be removed), basic grade only. Cultured cannot be reused.

### Bio Index
Cumulative sum of all installed bioware. Bioware does not cost Essence.

`Essence Index` :: current Essence + 3. No cyberware = Essence Index 9.

`Maximum Bio Index` :: 9 absolute. Exceeding 9: character dies.

A specific bioware implant can only be installed once per character.

### Bioware and Attribute Bonuses
Bioware attribute bonuses count as natural (unaugmented) base Attributes.

### Bioware and Magic
Magic reduction = Bio Index ÷ 2 (round down). Cumulative with Essence-based Magic loss.

Starting Magic = Essence − (Bio Index ÷ 2, round down).

### Bioware Drawbacks
`Lesser Immunity` :: +1 Power per 2 points Bio Index to any disease, drug, toxin, or compound resisted. Do not count bioware specifically protecting against those substances.

`Longer Healing Periods` :: Bio Index ÷ 2 (round up) added as TN modifier to all healing tests including magical.

### Excessive Bioware Drawbacks
*Applies when Bio Index ≥ Essence Index.*

`Easier to Die` :: Physical Overflow −1 per point (or portion) Bio Index exceeds Essence Index.

`Lesser Immunity (Excessive)` :: In addition to standard Power increase, −1 die Resistance Tests per 2 points (or part) Bio Index exceeds Essence Index.

`Longer Healing (Excessive)` :: −1 die Healing Tests per 2 points (or part) Bio Index exceeds Essence Index.

`System Overstress` :: Every bioware implant operates at Light Stress Level. Cannot be repaired or reversed.

### Bioware and Magic Effects

`Metabolic Suspension` :: Metabolic arrester + Hibernate spell + Suspended State adept power: only strongest applies.

`Pain Resistance` :: Damage compensators, pain editors, trauma dampers work against simulated-pain spells (Agony, Mass Agony). Pain Resistance adept power incompatible with pain editors and damage compensators. Trauma damper only affects damage Pain Resistance doesn't nullify. All pain-resisting bioware useless against Nerve Strike adept power.

`Reflex Boosters` :: If Reaction/Initiative-boosting bioware used with adept powers or spells: only highest bonus applies.

`Thermosense` :: Not vision-based; illusion spells targeting vision do not affect it. Critter power Concealment does hinder it.

### Slap Patches and Bioware
Blood filtration cyberware (kidney replacement, oral filters) blocks all patches except trauma. Bioware filters (Tracheal Filter, Toxin Extractor, Nephritic Screen) do not block patches by RAW.

*House rule: attack-vector patches (tranq) receive Nanosymbiote and Toxin Extractor Power reduction bonuses. Cooperative patches unaffected.*

## BASIC BIOWARE

### Adrenal Pump
Abdominal reservoir releasing adrenaline surge. Two levels. Roll 1D6 per level = Combat Turns active. Each level: +1 Quickness, +2 Strength, +1 Willpower, +2 Reaction (Quickness/Willpower affect Combat Pool; Reaction boost does not affect Control Pool; Quickness bonus does not affect Reaction). Normally triggers on Physical or Stun damage. Can trigger via ACTH drug; suppressed by MAO drug. After duration: Body vs Deadly Stun (Power = turns active). Recharge: 9+1D6 min; halved duration if reactivated before full. *Incompatible with move-by-wire.*

| Stress | Effect |
|---|---|
| Light | Twitching, uncontrollable shaking |
| Moderate | High blood pressure; −1 die Athletics |
| Serious | Roll 2D6 each activation; on 2: cardiac arrest — Deadly Physical, no resistance |
| Deadly | Cannot trigger voluntarily; activates randomly |

### Cat's Eyes
Light-amplifying replacement eyes. Low-light vision. Pupils slitted; mirror-like cornea; perceive color. Counts as natural (not cybernetic) for visibility modifiers. Incompatible with cybereyes and retinal modifications.

| Stress | Effect |
|---|---|
| Light | Difficulty distinguishing color; sensitive to bright light |
| Moderate | +1 modifier flash-paks/strobe effects |
| Serious | Black and gray only; +4 Perception to distinguish colors |
| Deadly | Essentially blinded (+8 all visual Perception and sight-requiring actions) |

### Chemical Gland
Produces one naturally occurring compound; stores up to 5 doses (1 dose per 24 hours). Cost: 30,000¥ + 100× one dose. Partial immunity to own compound. Four delivery methods:

`Exhalation Spray` :: Complex Action + Ranged Attack (Quickness). Range = ½ Body (round down, meters). Choke setting 2 (SR3 p. 117). No lingering cloud.
`Spit` :: Same test, no spread. Range = Body (meters); +1 TN/meter. Single target.
`Internal Release` :: Constant one dose in bloodstream.
`Weapon Reservoir` :: Adjacent to bladed cyberweapon or dart weapon; coats blade on retraction.

| Stress | Effect |
|---|---|
| Light | Gland irritated and swollen |
| Moderate | One dose per 48 hours only |
| Serious | Maximum 2 doses stored |
| Deadly | No compounds produced until host healed |

### Digestive Expansion
Stomach/pancreas/liver enhanced; taste/smell modified. Can consume almost anything organic. Lifestyle −20%. Immune to food poisoning. Ingested toxin Power halved (round down). Taste/smell Perception: −1 die. *Incompatible with ingested toxin filter cyberware.*

| Stress | Effect |
|---|---|
| Light | Heartburn, stomachaches, indigestion |
| Moderate | Toxin Power reduction only quarter |
| Serious | Ulcers — Light Physical and Stun wounds until treated |
| Deadly | Cannot hold food; Serious Physical; death by starvation in Body × 10 days |

### Enhanced Articulation
Joint/tendon/ligament augmentation. +1 die all Success Tests involving Combat, Physical, Technical, Build/Repair Skills. Physical use of Vehicle Skills (not DNI). +1 Reaction (no effect rigging/decking; does not affect Control Pool). Cannot be installed in cyberlimbs. All bonuses lost if >2 limbs replaced.

| Stress | Effect |
|---|---|
| Light | Muscle pains and cramps |
| Moderate | Tendonitis in one limb (GM); +1 modifier that limb |
| Serious | Pulled muscles; +1 TN all physical |
| Deadly | Joint pops out; +6 TN that limb; Serious wound; realignment: Biotech (6); after: +4 modifier until healed; leg: Movement halved, cannot run |

### Extended Volume
Augmented diaphragm. Three levels. Each: +45 seconds breath-holding; −1 modifier stamina/fatigue tests. *Incompatible with internal air tank cyberware.*

| Stress | Effect |
|---|---|
| Light | Hiccups, sleep apnea |
| Moderate | Body (8) under high exertion or −1 Willpower + Light Stun |
| Serious | Hernia; +1 modifier Strength/linked; any such test inflicts 6L damage |
| Deadly | Lungs partially collapse; Serious Physical until Stress reduced |

### Metabolic Arrester
Thyroid-regulating gland. Triggers on ≥10 boxes Physical damage: near-metabolic stasis (×5 metabolism, like Hibernate with 5 successes). Overflow: 1 box per (Body × 5) CTs. Biotech (4) to determine still alive. *Incompatible with suprathyroid gland and adrenal pump.*

| Stress | Effect |
|---|---|
| Light | Tired, needs more sleep |
| Moderate | Only slows metabolism ×2 |
| Serious | Triggers at Serious Physical damage; character falls unconscious |
| Deadly | Does not function |

### Muscle Augmentation
Vat-grown muscle cables braided into existing fibers. Four levels; each: +1 Strength (max +4). Compatible with muscle toner. *Incompatible with muscle replacement cyberware. Not in cyberlimbs.*

| Stress | Effect |
|---|---|
| Light | Muscle stiffness |
| Moderate | Tremors/spasms in one limb (GM); +1 modifier that limb |
| Serious | Inflamed ligaments/tendons; +1 modifier all physical |
| Deadly | Muscles tear; Strength = half unaugmented; +2 modifier all physical |

### Muscle Toner
Vat-grown elastic fibers. Four levels; each: +1 Quickness (max +4). Quickness bonus increases Reaction and Combat Pool (not Control Pool). Every 2 full levels: −1 modifier Athletics (Escape Artist). Compatible with muscle augmentation. *Incompatible with muscle replacement cyberware. Not in cyberlimbs.*

| Stress | Effect |
|---|---|
| Light | Muscle pains and cramps |
| Moderate | Tendonitis in one limb (GM); +1 modifier that limb |
| Serious | Inflamed/pulled muscles; +1 modifier all physical |
| Deadly | Tendons/ligaments rip; Quickness = half unaugmented; +2 modifier all physical |

### Nephritic Screen
Enhanced kidney filtration. +1 Body to resist toxins and pathogens. Reduces Power of pathogens and blood-vector toxins by 1.

| Stress | Effect |
|---|---|
| Light | Filters needed nutrients; may need supplements |
| Moderate | Power reduction no longer applies |
| Serious | Toxic mass leaking into system; Light Physical until cleaned (M&M p. 147) |
| Deadly | Stops filtering; Serious Physical until repaired |

### Nictitating Membranes
Clear inner eyelid. Light-sensitive; becomes tinted in bright light; polarized for glare. Reduces smoke and eye irritant modifiers by 1. Flare compensation for flashes and glare. Incompatible with cybereyes and flare compensation retinal modification. Prevents contact lenses.

| Stress | Effect |
|---|---|
| Light | Watery eyes |
| Moderate | +1 modifier low-light or worse |
| Serious | Membranes clouded; +1 Perception modifier |
| Deadly | Membranes rip; no bonuses until healed |

### Orthoskin
Sythagen and flextin grafted into dermal layer with modified cartilage flakes. Heals with little scarring. +level TN tactile Perception Tests. *Incompatible with dermal plating and sheathing. Not on cyberlimbs.*

| Level | Armor | Concealability |
|-------|-------|----------------|
| 1 | +1 Impact | 10 |
| 2 | +1 Impact, +1 Ballistic | 9 |
| 3 | +2 Impact, +1 Ballistic | 8 |

| Stress | Effect |
|---|---|
| Light | Dry and flaky skin |
| Moderate | Scars on damage; +2 modifier Body Test for scarring |
| Serious | Stiff and rigid; −1 Quickness |
| Deadly | Drastic eczema; Light wound; armor bonus −1 until healed |

### Pathogenic Defense
Enhanced leukocyte production. Variable levels (max = half unaugmented Body, round down). Reduces Power of biological attacks (disease, allergens, microbiologicals; not chemical) by 1 per level.

| Stress | Effect |
|---|---|
| Light | More vulnerable to common illness |
| Moderate | Leukemia; −1 die Body resisting disease |
| Serious | Leukemia intensifies; −1 die Body per level of pathogenic defense |
| Deadly | Chronic leukemia; no bonuses |

### Platelet Factories
Accelerated platelet production. Removes 1 box from any Moderate or higher Physical wound. Thrombosis risk: daily anticoagulant required (20¥ injected / 25¥ oral). Without anticoagulant: Body (3) every 12 hours, +1 TN/day without — fail: cardiac arrest/aneurysm (Deadly Physical, no resistance).

| Stress | Effect |
|---|---|
| Light | Poor circulation; cold extremities, tingling |
| Moderate | Blood clots; each day without Body (6) success: Light wound |
| Serious | −2 Body vs disease and infection |
| Deadly | Stroke; immediate Deadly Physical; possible long-term damage |

### Suprathyroid Gland
Supersedes thyroid metabolic functions. +1 all Physical Attributes and Reaction (Quickness bonus increases Reaction and Combat Pool; not Control Pool). Must ingest 2× food/drink. Lifestyle +40%. Generates extra heat: −1 TN thermographic detection; −1 Signature sensor/Gunnery Tests. *Incompatible with metabolic arrester.*

| Stress | Effect |
|---|---|
| Light | Hot and sweaty, weight loss |
| Moderate | Difficulty sleeping; +1 Perception modifier |
| Serious | Extreme fatigue; −1 all Mental Attributes |
| Deadly | Immediate cardiac arrest — Deadly Physical, no resistance |

### Symbiotes
Tailored microorganisms in bloodstream. Lifestyle +20%/level. Does not add Bio Index to bioware-extended healing calculations. Blood loss does not greatly offset function. Three levels. *Incompatible with nanosymbiotes nanoware.*

| Level | Healing Time |
|-------|-------------|
| 1 | 90% of normal |
| 2 | 70% of normal |
| 3 | 50% of normal |

| Stress | Effect |
|---|---|
| Light | Weight loss |
| Moderate | Increased heat; −1 TN thermographic; −1 Signature sensors/Gunnery |
| Serious | Bonus halved (95%/85%/75%) |
| Deadly | No healing bonus |

### Synthacardium
Enhanced myocardium. Two levels; each: +1 die Athletics Tests; extra die cardiac/circulatory resistance tests.

| Stress | Effect |
|---|---|
| Light | Acute migraines |
| Moderate | Hypertension; roll 2D6 each Athletics — on 2: stroke (paralysis, sense/mental loss at GM discretion) |
| Serious | Chest pains; Light Stun until healed |
| Deadly | Bonuses no longer apply |

### Tailored Pheromones
Altered sweat glands. Species-specific (all metatypes). Two levels; each: +1 die Charisma and Social Skill Tests. No effect conjuring or astral. Range ~15–20 m diameter. No effect on impaired olfactory or astral travelers. Not detectable by standard metahuman senses. Cultured grade: double benefits; range +50%.

Detection: TN 10; −4 TN if triggered; −1 TN during heavy exertion.

| Stress | Effect |
|---|---|
| Light | Leaves more lasting impression than intended |
| Moderate | Halved area of effect |
| Serious | 50% chance pheromones have opposite effect per person |
| Deadly | No bonuses |

### Thermosense Organ
Detects heat. Not vision-based — vision illusion spells (Invisibility) do not affect it; critter power Concealment does.

| Condition | TN |
|---|---|
| Clear | 4 |
| Lightly obscured | 6 |
| Heavily obscured | 8 |
| Thermal smoke | +6 |

| Stress | Effect |
|---|---|
| Light | Headaches and unplaced sensations |
| Moderate | False positives; constantly detects nonexistent heat |
| Serious | +2 modifier all Perception Tests |
| Deadly | Fails entirely |

### Toxin Extractor
Specialized liver cell cluster. Variable levels (max = half unaugmented Body, round down). Reduces Power of blood-borne toxin attacks by 1 per level. *House rule: applies to attack-vector patches (tranq) received as attacks.*

| Stress | Effect |
|---|---|
| Light | Filters needed nutrients; may need supplements |
| Moderate | −1 die Body Resistance Tests vs toxins/poisons |
| Serious | Leaks toxic particles; Light Physical + blisters/rashes |
| Deadly | Stops filtering; no bonuses |

### Tracheal Filter
Traps airborne impurities at top of trachea. Variable levels (max = half unaugmented Body, round down). Reduces Power of air-vector attacks by level. Does not stop bacterial-sized impurities. *Incompatible with air filtration system cyberware (SR3 p. 300) and OXSYS cybergill.*

| Stress | Effect |
|---|---|
| Light | Constant mucus; coughing fits |
| Moderate | Swelling/irritation; +2 modifier Athletics |
| Serious | Half effectiveness (round down) |
| Deadly | Filter shuts down; severe larynx swelling; Complex Action to speak a short phrase |

### Trauma Damper
Specialized receptor cluster at thalamus base. Releases endorphins/enkephalins on trauma. When Physical damage inflicted: shift 1 box Physical → Stun. When Stun inflicted: −1 box. −2 Open Test painful interrogation. −2 TN pain resistance tests. Not compatible with active pain editor. With damage compensators: applies only when compensators' capacity exceeded.

| Stress | Effect |
|---|---|
| Light | Mood swings; weight loss |
| Moderate | Delay — effects applied at end of CT damage received |
| Serious | Further delay — effects applied end of CT following damage |
| Deadly | No bonuses |

## CULTURED BIOWARE

### Cerebral Booster
Extra nerve tissue in frontal lobes. Two levels; each: +1 Intelligence (affects Reaction and dice pools). Level 2 also: Task Pool 1 (Intelligence-linked skills only).

| Stress | Effect |
|---|---|
| Light | Acute headaches |
| Moderate | ADD; −1 die all concentration tasks including magical |
| Serious | Frequently zones out; +2 modifier all Perception Tests |
| Deadly | Once/day: 2-in-6 chance epileptic seizure — 8D Stun, convulses 2D6 minutes, loses surroundings awareness |

### Damage Compensators
Implanted nerve fiber ribbons bypassing safety inhibitors. Nine levels. Subtract level from damage boxes before determining injury modifiers. Applies equally to Physical and Stun. GM may track damage secretly; Perception (6) or Biotech (4) to reveal Damage Level.

| Stress | Effect |
|---|---|
| Light | Phantom pain from previous wounds |
| Moderate | +1 TN all Healing Tests |
| Serious | Only half level (round down) applies |
| Deadly | Does not function |

### Mnemonic Enhancer
Gray matter growth for enhanced memory. Three levels.

| Level | Effects |
|---|---|
| 1 | +1 die Language Skill Tests; +1 die memory-related Intelligence Tests |
| 2 | +2 dice; defaulting Knowledge Skill to Intelligence: +3 TN instead of +4 |
| 3 | +3 dice; +1 die all Knowledge Skill Tests; +1 die language-related Intelligence Tests |

Karma cost to learn/improve skills and specializations: −1 (minimum 1). Does not apply to skillsoft skills.

| Stress | Effect |
|---|---|
| Light | Headaches and déjà vu |
| Moderate | Memories surface randomly; −1 die concentration including spellcasting |
| Serious | (Level) times/day: Willpower (6) or flashback — out of touch 1D6 minutes |
| Deadly | Making Knowledge/Language Test: Willpower (6) or distracted; +2 all actions (+4 concentration) for 2D6 minutes |

### Pain Editor
Filters sensory stimuli. Activation/deactivation: learned reflex. While active: cannot perceive pain; GM tracks damage secretly. Ignores Initiative and TN penalties from Stun damage. Physical penalties applied without player's knowledge. Will not knock unconscious from Stun (may from Deadly Physical). +1 Willpower; −1 Intelligence; +4 TN tactile Perception. On deactivation: all damage effects apply immediately.

| Stress | Effect |
|---|---|
| Light | Pain sensitivity randomly fluctuates |
| Moderate | Occasionally dampens another sense (GM selects); +1 Perception modifier |
| Serious | More sensitive to pain; increase Damage Level by one for wound TN modifiers |
| Deadly | Fails to activate |

### Reflex Recorder
Extra neural material around spinal nerve clusters. Each recorder: +1 die to one specific Combat or Physical Skill (chosen at implantation; cannot change except by surgical reprogramming). One per skill; not stackable. Multiple recorders for different skills permitted. Large (base skill) and small (specialization) sizes. Does not apply to skillsoft skills.

| Stress | Effect |
|---|---|
| Light | Unwanted reflexive actions matching the skill |
| Moderate | +1 additional modifier when defaulting to that skill |
| Serious | *(source truncated)* |
| Deadly | *(source truncated)* |

### Synaptic Accelerator
Cultured grade. Two levels; each: +1D6 Initiative (physical only — not rigging or decking). Does not affect Reaction calculation. *Incompatible with wired reflexes and move-by-wire.*

*Stress effects not in source — handle under stress module.*

## COSMETIC BIOWARE
*Primarily roleplaying vehicle. GM may apply social situational impacts. Stress effects at GM discretion.*

### Chloroplast Skin
Skin turns green; provides supplemental food via photosynthesis (vitamins/nutrients still required). *Incompatible with orthoskin and skin pigmentation.*

### Clean Metabolism
Minimizes body odor, digestive effects; sanitized sweat and waste. Reduces scent detection range; Pheromone Detection TN 10. *Incompatible with digestive expansion.*

### Dietware
Limits fat storage regardless of intake. Cannot gain excess weight. *Incompatible with digestive expansion.*

### Hair Growth
Grows hair in excess or unusual spots. Each treatment covers head-sized skin area. Texture, color, thickness chosen in advance.

### Scent Glands
Changes exuded scent and sweat taste to any selection from wide market range. Scent chosen in advance.

### Sensitive Skin
Increases dermal nerve receptors. Heightens sensitivity to heat, cold, pressure, and pain. *Incompatible with orthoskin and dermal plating/sheathing.*

### Skin Pigmentation and Bio-Tattoos
Alters melanin; wide color range, entire body or selected areas. Bio-tattoos don't fade; can change slowly over time. *Incompatible with chloroplast skin. Iris alteration incompatible with cybereyes.*
