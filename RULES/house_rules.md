## Bioware & Geneware House Rules

### Genetailoring
Treated as trans-implant surgery applied to genetic modification. Special option
to allow genetech options not otherwise available. Requires a fully vat-grown body
with integral bioware.

### Abnormal Oxygen Adaptation
Similar to Low Oxygen Adaptation. Allows a wider range of oxygen tolerance without
extreme physiological impacts.

### Improved Glucagon
Similar to standard Glucagon bioware, adapted to have less direct benefit and
eliminate dangerous side effects. Required to interact properly with other bioware
in the package.

## Adept / Magic House Rules

### Infinite Warehouse
*Special ability — metaplanar storage*

Allows physical items to be moved into a metaplane for storage. Restoring items
from the metaplane uses the same action as placing them.

- May place anything up to Max Lift × 10 in mass
- Unlimited volume and mass capacity in storage
- Both parties must be present for retrieval if item was placed by another

**Action costs by mass:**

| Mass | Action Required |
|------|----------------|
| 0–100 kg | Free Action |
| 101–1,000 kg | Simple Action |
| 1,001+ kg | Complex Action |
| Over Max Lift × 10 | Extended Action — 10 × Max Lift × Combat Turns (Concentration) |
