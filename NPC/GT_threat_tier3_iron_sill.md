## GM Notes

**The capture priority:**
GT wants the 3PR intact. The augmentation load, magical integration, and initiate grade represent an investment that cannot be replicated at any known price. Lethal force is a last resort and requires GT principal authorization above Reyes.

**The BattleTac gap:**
IS-1 has a TC but Singer's system is superior in every parameter. IS-1 will recognize this within one exchange and adjust tactics away from direct confrontation.

**The magical ceiling:**
IS-6 at Grade 3 will assense the family and recognize he is looking at something extraordinary. Grade 8 masked auras are not blank walls to him — he can see there is masking, he cannot pierce it. He will report: *"Grade 8 minimum, possibly higher. Three of them. I am not equipped for this."* He withdraws. He does not engage.

**The escalation brake:**
Iron Sill deployment requires GT principal authorization — Reyes cannot order this alone. The principal deciding to deploy Iron Sill means GT has abandoned the quiet approach entirely. This is a visible commitment with consequences if it fails.

**If Iron Sill fails:**
GT has no Tier 4. They escalate to political and legal pressure, or they cut losses and bury the HMS thread entirely. The signatory either goes to ground or becomes someone else's problem.
