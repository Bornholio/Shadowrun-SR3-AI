## CAMPAIGN OVERVIEW

**Game:** Shadowrun 3rd Edition (SR3) — SR3 Companion rules apply for edges
**Setting:** Seattle Metroplex, 2055
**Group Name:** Malice (the "La" is dropped as superfluous)
**Shadow Reputation / Web Presence:** Orthogonal Gate
**Premise:** Seven elven runners — a family unit — wake up in a McHugh's diner
on the Auburn/Seattle border after an unknown medical procedure they cannot
remember. Their memories are fragmented — they remember their lives clearly
only up to 2025. They have a limited set of gear and a set of contacts they
don't recognize.

## SR3 HOUSE RULES IN USE

- Weekly Good Karma: 1 GK per week for 8+ hours of genuine positive community
  building activity
- Edge cost: 10 GK per build point
- Language base pool: INT × 1.5 (rounded down)
- Bilingual edge: 2 native languages free at INT × 1 each
- Language R/W: spoken ÷ 2 (rounded down) free by default
- Exception: Dennis, Frances, Brian, Kim, Angela get English R/W = INT
  (pre-computer literacy generation). Gabe and Mike do not.
- Lingos/Jargon: treated as skill specializations of root language, not
  separate skills
- Mnemonic Enhancer 3 discount: -3 GK per language learned post-creation
  (minimum 1 GK)
- R/W at half spoken is free when learning languages with ME discount
- Good Reputation 2: -2 TN on social tests where reputation known OR felt.
  Fully cancels +2 wariness/suspicion modifiers. All 7 get baseline
  trustworthiness read even from strangers.
- Stat maximum: racial max + all modifiers
- Stat ceiling: adjusted maximum × 1.5 (rounded down)
- Tailored Pheromones 2: +4 effective Charisma for social tests within 20-30m
  (not a permanent stat change)
- Synaptic Accelerator 2: +2d6 Initiative (SR3 rule)
- Muscle Toner 4: +4 Quickness, -2 TN Athletics (including Escape Artist)
- Awakened Bioindex: older rule — BI counts as direct Essence loss
- Surgical reduction: -90% Essence/BI cost achieved via karma expenditure
- Quick Healer: -2 TN on healing tests (pending book verification)
- Natural Aptitude: -1 TN to specific skill
- Perceptive: -1 TN Perception tests / +1 open Perception tests
- Initiative passes: count down in decades, highest first, subtract 10 each
  pass, 0 or below = no action. Movement divided across passes.

## ORIGIN STORY

### Elven Expression
All seven are first-wave elven metamorphosis expressions — among the earliest.
They expressed in the central states where being newly expressed elves was
dangerous and difficult. During this period all seven organized and helped
others going through genetic expression — 8-10 hours per week of genuine
charitable work sustained over years. This is the source of Good Reputation 2
for all of them.

### Harlequin's Back
All seven were present at the adventure Harlequin's Back. They participated in
blunting the early arrival of the Horrors. The magical backlash from that
working, combined with the energy released, created a localized magic surge
that triggered the second genetic expression in all seven.

### Night One / SURGE Expression
Dennis, Frances, Brian, Kim, and Gabe are Night Ones — their second expression
came as a SURGE event layered on top of their initial elven metamorphosis.
Mike and Angela have less severe SURGE changes but still expressed. Common
family SURGE traits to be listed when character sheets are available.

### The Surgery (3PR)
Brian, Kim, and Gabe (the 3PR — Prime Runners) underwent extraordinary surgery:
- Over 9 Essence worth of Delta-grade cyberware and bioware installed
- Surgeon's own result: -60% Essence cost, +2 Concealment rating
- Karma expenditure improved surgical outcome to -90% Essence cost
- Total Essence loss: approximately 1.0 (cyberware ~0.5 + bioindex ~0.5)
- Genetic modification post-SURGE adds to Bioindex with similar surgical
  results
- Pre-modification Karma Pool: 56 points total per 3PR character
- Karma spent on surgical outcomes: 45 points each
- Remaining Karma Pool: 11 points each
- Magic 12 remains despite heavy augmentation — cyber and magic integrated
  rather than in conflict. The surgery achieved something with no published
  precedent.

### The Missing Years
All seven remember their lives clearly only up to 2025. The shadow years
(2025-2055) are inaccessible — they have the skills, hardware, and muscle
memory of thirty years of shadowrunning without the conscious memories.
The amnesia is the central mystery of the campaign.

### Adventure History (3PR)
Brian, Kim, and Gabe have run through essentially all SR1-3 published
adventures up to 2055. Their exceptional stats, skills, and karma totals
reflect a full career.

### Karma Economy
- Dennis and Frances: ~500 good karma earned in post-sinless periods
- Mike and Angela: ~120 good karma
- 3PR: substantial — full adventure career plus weekly karma
- All seven: 1 GK/week for community building throughout
- 10 GK per edge build point explains exceptional edge selection

Back through the secondary script's linguistic roots.
