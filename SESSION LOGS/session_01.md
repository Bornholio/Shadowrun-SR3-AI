## Setting

**Location:** McHugh's Diner, Auburn/Seattle border, Puyallup Barrens edge
**Date:** ~March 2058 — exact date unknown. Morning.
**Situation:** Seven elves wake up post-medical procedure with fragmented memory.
Last clear memories: 2025. The shadow years (2025–2058) are inaccessible —
skills, hardware, and muscle memory intact. Conscious memories gone.

## Key NPCs Encountered

**Siobhan** — Elven girl behind the McHugh's counter. Dark hair, two silver rings
in pointed ears. Has been in Auburn three years. Watched the group with careful
neutrality. Knows what an unmarked medical transport on Route 167 means. Mike left
her a 20¥ tip. She saw them leave under Concealment and her eyes slid off them —
she knows what that means too.

### Chip Contents (Original Device)
- 11 hours 34 minutes of recording — device placed approximately 2100 previous night
- **0152:** Single elven figure, hood up, enters McHugh's 4 minutes, exits.
  Pauses at doorframe 2 seconds — places the glyph. Knows where device is,
  doesn't acknowledge it.
- **0340:** Unmarked panel van, no plates. Side door opens. 7 figures assisted
  out — post-sedation, moving slowly. Enter McHugh's. Van departs north.
  Under 3 minutes total.
- **0603:** Family exits. Concealment visible as wrongness in footage.
  LS officers look away on cue. Fog rolls in 90 seconds later.

### Device Examination (Gabe's Organic Microscopic Vision)
- Tooling marks: custom machined, small run, artisan-level, tighter than military spec
- Material: organometallic alloy — partially grown rather than purely smelted
- Secondary glyph: hair-thin, etched inside stamp, OLD SCRIPT — related to
  Sperethiel but older. Kim partially reads: WATCHER/GUARDIAN and RETURNED/REMEMBERED/AWAKE
- Fingerprint in adhesive: deliberate, pressed with fingertip as it set. Calling card.
- Same glyph as doorframe below — same entity placed both

### Brian's Realization
- Sees through Gabe's eyes via BattleTac simsense feed
- Kim and Brian both find VCR in their hardware simultaneously
- 3PR are on each other's subscriber lists — pre-configured
- Heavy encryption, 250m range, minimal flux — deliberate specification
- Drone blackout order has second explanation: 3PR VCRs could commandeer
  any drone within 250m
- Brian runs cyber-magic math: Magic 12 + this augmentation load = impossible
  under standard SR3 physics. TC flags ANOMALOUS — no clean category.
- Karma Pool shadow: 45 of 56 points pre-spent. Cannot be spent by another —
  the 3PR chose this. All three same shadow.
- Cyber and magic INTEGRATED not competing — the surgery achieved something
  with no published name
- Says out loud: "Not sure why us three, but my first bad guess is they were
  making us cyber zombies and failed." Shakes head. Knows it's wrong.
- Mike: "Why us then?" Nobody answers.
- Frances: "Get him back. We need to move."

### Going Dark — Squatter Building

  minutes longer as gift
- Mike leads through gap between buildings (feet know the way)
- Former light industrial unit — current occupant absent, Stiletto-adjacent territory

- WNG R6 covers interior — electronically silent
- Gabe discovers full bioware picture: muscle toner (Quickness), muscle aug (Strength),

- Wired Reflexes NOT installed — would conflict with Synaptic Accelerator
- Reaction Enhancers 6 confirmed
- Brian: "Don't go looking for [the reflex trigger] in the middle of the street."
- Family goes still for first time since McHugh's
