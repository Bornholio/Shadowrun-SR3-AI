### Night — Banshee Solo (0213–0230)

Banshee surfaced at 0213 — Wolf's ears turned toward the street and water simultaneously. Something on 3rd was different.

**Park — watcher spirit**
Astral shifted as great green flower wolf, masking eased. Found F3 hermetic watcher at Belltown Corridor park perimeter — passive, planted, reporting type. Controlled it (9 net successes, no drain damage). Interrogated:

- Summoner: male, elven, older, silver hair worn long. Hermetic bearing. Calm and patient. Clothing not Seattle — different cut. 4th World resonance in his aura matching the organometallic key and glyph devices.
- Wolf recognized him below the reset. *You have met this man before.*
- Same workshop as the key, the glyph devices, Reyes's burned node components. Confirmed.

Redirected watcher's remaining 7 services to help the sleepers on the benches until it saw them eat. Watcher faded during the day, services expended, per GM close.

**Flurry's trail**
Tracked Flurry's dual-natured path from safehouse toward the waterfront. Trail jogged east one block then corrected — something drew her off the direct line. Filed for later.

**Pier — the thing in the water**
Followed 4th World resonance to the pier. Assensed the structure:
- Organometallic resonance distributed through the pier material itself — older than the current structure, survived at least one rebuild
- Passive receiver, not broadcaster. Tuned to the "old blood" elven signature specifically — keyed to the family
- No physical component removable — distributed through the material like dye

Astral Shifted to physical (6M drain, 2 boxes Stun). Arrived barefoot in sleepwear.

**Conversation with the thing in the water:**
Large. Dual-natured. Seal-shaped in the way Dag is wolverine-shaped — the form is a suggestion. Aura predates the Awakening magically. Puget Sound nativity, old depth, long patience.

- Responded to Kéet and proto-Sperethiel address
- Confirmed: same pale architect placed the watcher in the park and built the receiver into the pier
- *"Not the one who watches from buildings. Older. The one who built the listening into the wood."*
- Has been waiting for the family to stand on this pier with bare feet and speak the deep name — this was the instruction left by the pale architect
- *"He said you would come. He did not say when."* Duration of waiting: at least 40 years, possibly longer
- The glyph: *"That mark is a door. You are what it was made for."*
- The pier, the receiver, the listening — all built for the family specifically
- Has things it was not given leave to say — a real constraint from the pale architect, not evasion
- Parting gift: image of the pale architect standing on this pier, younger, waiting for something to surface. Same expression Banshee had.
- Accepted farewell in proto-Sperethiel with evident pleasure — had been a long time since anyone spoke to it properly

Returned physically with Flurry. Astral Shifted back to bedroom (6M drain, 2 more boxes Stun).

**Drain total end of session:** 3 boxes Stun (1 from F8 summoning + 2 from Astral Shift return)
