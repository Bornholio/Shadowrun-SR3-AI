## Session Events

The session opened with a week of downtime. Keystone, Meridian, Crowbar, and Kluger searched for day jobs over six days — all four found positions, all cash, all SINless-friendly. Each earning 250¥/week. Carpenter and Banshee built structured history curriculum from matrix education services; all seven completed a knowledge learning test establishing trusted academic ceiling ratings (Singer/Banshee/Carpenter at 5, Meridian at 4, Keystone/Kluger at 3, Crowbar at 2). Four day-job characters retired out of active sessions.

Singer cracked four LS patrol cells and the DocWagon channel using shift-change timing. LS cells required minimum successes at the edge — shift change was the margin. DW channel cracked in seven minutes due to lax monthly cipher rotation. Banshee took the DW channel for ongoing monitoring, Carpenter mapped LS patrol patterns with camera overlay, Singer monitored the detective band.

From the monitoring: Detective Sergeant Mara Okonkwo (Special Investigations) referenced "the Auburn thing" and the word "Paladin" with her junior partner. The BOLO officer is on shift but not pursuing. An unreadable burst transmission at 0412 from a Belltown/SLU border building was logged — non-standard encryption, non-department hardware.

Banshee pulled civil camera footage from the 0340 DW incident. A dark Eurocar Westwind was staged outside, present for 47+ minutes before the team arrived. A non-livery male elf — trained, 182–184cm, receiving instructions post-event — attended as client liaison and departed separately from the team. A dark sedan departed the area afterward.

Three deckers ran to a matrix parlor and coffin hotel. Established a persistent BT matrix link group via the tower management host. Planted an LTG spoof trigger on the primary Belltown tower — fires on Singer's RC command, reroutes traffic to Queen Anne/Uptown LTG. Passive alert triggered on exit; all three logged off clean before IC engaged.

Vehicle transponder research identified the partial plate as a deliberate DMV poison pill — planted to flag investigators and point at Meridian's cover SIN. Someone with knowledge of the family's current identity architecture planted the trap. Carpenter parity-spoofed both the DMV entry and the transponder anomaly; host will restore clean backup data within 6–12 hours.

A commlink trap was planted in a Spokane vending machine host — any query chain touching Meridian's cover SIN logs the querying commcode to Singer's dead line.

Banshee's camera sweep located a dark Westwind at 2nd and Lenora at 0612, heading north — six blocks from Sandy Systems. The family moved out: three 3PR in civilian clothes, staggered spacing, concealment active.

Three Great Form City Spirits summoned and invoked on the street — no drain across the board. Wind Spirit F8 summoned by Banshee. On round three all three channeled their spirits — again no drain. The wind spirit located the Westwind parked cold on a side street off 1st Ave. Singer entered the vehicle: found the case, the hidden compartment, and a navigation log showing Auburn South four days pre-wakeup. He took RC control of the Westwind via the RC deck link.

Carpenter identified a single watcher in a panel van at the north end of the pier — one occupant, mundane, facing the pier not the car. A single individual was on foot heading toward the waterfront. The family split: Singer secured the vehicle and items, Carpenter and Banshee pursued.

At the pier: a human male, mid-40s, trained, deliberately unarmed, waiting. He spoke first — *"I was wondering when you'd make it this far."* Banshee held silence. Singer arrived and assessed: *"Feels like a petulant dilettante playing with toys."* The man absorbed it. Banshee directed both to the van. Carpenter removed the driver door — Singer reattached it with Fix Vehicle, factory finish, no drain.

An LS-adjacent tracker was found on the van chassis — 48 hours old. The pier man did not appear to know it was there. It was dropped on the floor in front of the driver without comment. The man's response confirmed he'd suspected something like it.

The family faded with Invisibility spells, issued the Westwind a navigation command to a nearby parking structure, and withdrew clean.

Back at the safehouse: full microscopic and electronic sweep of all items. The proto-Sperethiel note and data chip two are unreadable — same script family as the Session 01 glyph. The key is organometallic, same material as the glyph hardware, astrally loud with ancient resonance. The credstick reads to the Herbertson Medical Services corporate port standard. The Callum Shire SIN card carries the pier man's most recent handling aura.

Carpenter sent a message to the pier man's commcode — a photograph of an elf on a gurney with *"whats this"*. Response: a photograph of the 3PR surgery. One word: *"yours."*

Cross-referencing the 0340 footage against the pier man: no match — the non-livery figure at the incident was a male elf, the pier man is human. The Callum Shire SIN — elf male — connects circumstantially.

Singer ran DocWagon's data store — Orange host, cracked clean with a graceful logoff as Killer-6 arrived. Three data points extracted:

1. The 0340 incident was billed to account **HMS-7731-CORP** — Herbertson Medical Services. The account is still active.
2. The non-livery client liaison at the incident was logged as **Callum Shire** — the name on the SIN card the pier man handled most recently.
3. One of the three SRT medics on the Delta team was **Oswin** — the same Oswin from Session 06, former SRT, SINless, Belltown sector, F&V Baptist commcode holder, flagged kit serial he doesn't know about.

Someone has been operating under the family's corporate name while they were asleep.
