**Memory test TN11:** 1 suc. Fragment surfaces — cold astral space, someone present who was not threat, flower-names in Sperethiel as identification. No face, no name. The feeling of something reliable.

**Banshee casts Mind Probe F5 while speaking.** 6 suc vs Aldric WIL resistance 4 suc — probe establishes, thin margin. **He allows it.** No counter, no shield.

Probe reads: relief genuine and structural, no deception present anywhere in emotional substrate around the key, the case, the family. Urgency underneath — patient, managed, now pressing. Protectiveness directed specifically at all seven. Surface thought readable because he permits it: *"Please let them have kept the key safe."*

Flurry called from Unit 5 with key tied to collar. Arrives four minutes.

**Aldric identifies himself.** *"I am called Aldric. I have been called other things, in other times."*

On the police: *"Bureaucratic residue. Someone filed something they should not have filed."* Okonkwo assessed as not the threat — persistent, good, will find them eventually regardless. *"The question is what she finds when she does."*

On Reyes: managing someone. Protecting someone. Deliberate withholding on the name — not deception, a decision. Same emotional signature around that name throughout the conversation.

On the chip: *"Someone touched that chip before it reached him. Someone with considerably more magical sophistication than GT Security Division employs."* Same withholding.

Aldric sees Flurry: *"Hello, old friend."* Root Sperethiel. To the cat, not Banshee. Flurry stops, sits, looks at him. Aldric does not reach for the key. *"It belongs to you. Not to me. I have only been keeping the door."*

Banshee: *"Let's investigate the door then."*

Aldric produces card — old paper, same hand as pier note. Two lines proto-Sperethiel, Tacoma waterfront address, specific berth number, one word: *"Wednesday."*

*"Sunrise. All seven if possible."*
*"There are things you need to understand before others find you who will explain them differently."*

Probe substrate: genuine fear of what happens if someone else gets there first.

Banshee takes the card. All three headware archive the image. Aldric acknowledges Singer and Carpenter's invisible positions — *"The northeast corner covers the approach I would use if I intended harm. Good instinct."*

Key instruction: *"Keep it on you. Not stored. Not in a bag. On your person."*

On Drake: *"You handled the acquisition team tonight with considerable efficiency. Drake will be curious about you for some time. That is not entirely unwelcome."*

Aldric walks out of the alley. Gone in thirty seconds.
