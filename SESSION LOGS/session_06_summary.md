## Play Session — Belltown Morning (~0549–1200)

### Departure — 0549
3PR depart Sandy Systems Unit 5 in cover. Disguise loadout: wraparound eyewear, beanies with ears tucked, muted clothing, armor jackets, ponchos (green/gray, rain), collapsible staves in umbrella holsters, pistols concealed. Baptist patch and red cross under jacket flap. Masks up. Sobriquets only — no street names. Pheromones off. No magic, no spirits. Banshee astrally peeks occasionally; all three fully masked, mundane to anything below Grade 8.

Patrol spacing: 10–12 meters, lead/backup/overwatch. Fast when unobserved, civilian pace when seen.

Singer on point. TC painting patrol pattern — one car unit left in sector, 23-minute circuit. Window clean.

### Go Gang Diversion — 0638
Disassembler/Halloweener running fight erupts on 1st Ave South, waterfront approach. One Halloweener with flamethrower rig on bike. LS sector thins — all car units diverted, foot units pulled to cordon. Belltown gap opens: one car unit on rotation, response times 20–30 minutes. Window live approximately 0638–0930.

### Marta — 0615 (Parking Structure / 1st Ave Alley)
Woman logged at 0104 (LS overnight intercept) sitting on Bell Street. Identified in doorway near parking structure — bruised jaw and left arm, bag missing. Roll crew (Kemp and Sutter) still in gray Americar on parking structure level 3, bag visible on back seat.

Singer observed car occupants (thermographic, 6 successes) — passenger (Sutter) confirmed right-hip carry, driver (Kemp) unarmed above seat level. Both in light sleep.

Banshee approached Marta — ministry cover, hand contact, sympathy register. Marta confirmed: two men in gray car, took her bag two hours prior, knew the camera on level 3 was offline. Not their first time.

Banshee cast Heal F4 on Marta (bruising, split lip, arm) — full healing, negligible drain. Marta confirmed 340¥ credstick and a burner comm with one critical contact number were in the bag.

Singer bluffed Lone Star Detective at the car window — pheromones at full, Intimidation + CHA 15. Sutter made a weapon reach and caught himself. Singer called "Bag" with 6-success Small Unit Tactics timing cue to Carpenter on TC.

Carpenter (Invisibility F3, Clubs 6, surprise attack) struck Sutter's right forearm from behind — arm compromised, weapon stay holstered. Carpenter removed bag from back seat. Directed Kemp to drive to the waterfront pier and remain until 0800.

Kemp complied without negotiation. Sutter's pistol kicked under the car.

Singer wiped and dropped Kemp's SIN card, texted LS non-emergency lost-and-found with location and plate image (Commlink 8, Spoof running). Tagged full simsense recording to Extender Box — compressed, encrypted, stored.

Carpenter checked bag: all contents intact, nothing missing. Added F&V Baptist commcode to Marta's burner (contact: F&V Baptist). Bag returned to Marta.

Marta volunteered: shelter contact on 3rd doing SINless intake paperwork, holds paperwork for people who need to disappear from LS records before processing. Offered as reciprocal exchange. Two camp members still scattered — location unknown.

Banshee approached alone with Singer's coffee — ministry register, no pressure, *null sheen* City Speak. Package visible as stiffness in jacket lining. Banshee read it, said nothing.

Lace arrived 0847, searched the alley for 90 seconds, made two calls, departed south 0854 without making contact.

Biometric data from gray-market DW unit treatment (2211 Session 05) queued in broker system — hit generated ~1100. Buyer unknown. Thread dormant.

### Waterfront Pier — 0901 (Oswin and Rat)
Post-gang-fight. Family approached pier in tourist cover — cheap caps, amber glasses, Korean makeup style on Banshee, tourist map (Singer), coffee cup (Carpenter). Etiquette 4 successes each — holds at distance, doesn't hold at close range for trained observers.

**Oswin:** Human male, mid-50s, SINless, former DocWagon SRT paramedic. Lost SIN in creditor action 3 years prior — disputed negligence claim, patient lived, case closed (he thinks). Resoled Magnums, WR1 installed. Trauma kit with DocWagon serial number (flagged in pending internal investigation — Oswin unaware). On his knees under the pier searching systematically for the kit.

Banshee approached: *"Looking for something?"* Oswin confirmed kit description and location, identified Rat's position under the walkway. Read Banshee's movement as non-civilian immediately. Accepted the Baptist patch without comment.

Carpenter spotted kit from deck — cross-brace junction, 4 pilings out. Retrieved with one-handed deck-edge reach.

Banshee called Rat up: *"Hey omae, you all right, I'll get medical to you, null sheen."* Rat confirmed he was present. Banshee entered pier understructure — movement read as exceptional by Oswin, who observed without comment.

**Rat:** Human male, ~17, Disassembler prospect, south end crew. Cousin got him the prospect slot. Hiding under walkway with forearm burn (flamethrower proximity blast) layered over an existing brand (1–2 weeks old, linear, deliberate — house mark profile).

**Banshee — Heal F6:**
Full Spell Pool (8 casting / 4 drain). Centering Mode C — Latin, *"Fiat lux."* Multiple successes, TN reduced to floor. Sorcery 6 + 8 pool vs TN 2 (Rat's Body 3, centering applied). 14 dice. Full healing: burn closed, blistering resolved, third-degree patch at wrist normalized. Brand — gone. 12 seconds. Drain: WIL 14 + 4 pool vs TN 3 — no drain taken.

Rat watched his arm for the rest of the scene. Had no framework for what happened.

Oswin observed the heal from 18 inches. Folded the unused burn dressing and returned it to the kit without comment.

Singer dropped F&V Baptist commcode card to Oswin — no speech, two sentences: *"Rollers will not be operating in DT."* Filed with a nod.

Singer dropped F&V Baptist commcode card to Rat: *"Hide your colors, kid. Cops will be by not too long from now."* Rat turned his lapel over, buttoned the top button. Left at a walk. Looked back once from the top of the stairs.

Banshee to Oswin on the pier deck: established cover as medical ministry, all three are medicos, awakened specialist. Oswin recalibrated without making a production of it. Confirmed: former SRT, sector 3 years, independent now. Knows the gray-market DW unit on 3rd (stolen livery, garage, not in official logs). Has the F&V Baptist commcode.

*"Rollers will not be operating in DT"* — Singer, departing, smug.
*"We still need a cat box and litter"* — Carpenter, already composing a shopping list.
