# SR3 Critter Powers Reference
*Malice Family Campaign*
*File location: RULES/critters_powers_sr3.md*
*Source: Shadowrun Third Edition core rulebook — Powers of the Awakened*

---

## General Rules

**Shared state requirement:** A creature must share the same state as its target — astral or physical. Solely astral forms cannot affect solely physical targets and vice versa. An astral form and a dual-natured being can affect each other (mana-based powers only). A physical form and a dual-natured being can affect each other (both mana and physical powers).

**Power types:**
- **Mana (M)** — affects mind, emotions, life force, or spirit; can affect astral targets
- **Physical (P)** — affects the physical world; cannot be used without physical presence

**Actions:** Complex, Simple, Free, or Automatic (Auto = always on, no action required). Exclusive powers cannot be used while sustaining any other power — must drop other sustained effects first.

**Range:** Line of Sight (LOS), Touch, or Self. Targeted as spells. Nature spirit powers only function within the spirit's domain.

**Targeting:** Powers generally affect one target per action unless noted otherwise.

**Duration:** Instant, Sustained, Permanent, Always, or Special.
- Always: Auto powers, constantly in effect
- Instant: takes effect and vanishes; lasting effects (damage) remain
- Sustained: maintained over time at no effort or cost; no strain or TN modifier; damage does not disrupt; LOS not required to maintain (except domain-limited spirits); a spirit can sustain powers equal to its Essence simultaneously
- Permanent: effect becomes permanent after a set time; see individual power
- Special: duration depends on other factors; see individual power

## Powers — Alphabetical

---

### ACCIDENT
*Type: P · Action: Complex · Range: LOS · Duration: Instant*

Target makes Quickness or Intelligence test (whichever greater) TN = critter's Essence. Failure = target loses next Initiative Pass (trips, stumbles, environmental interference). Not inherently damaging but environment can make it lethal (cliff edge, traffic, etc.). Against vehicles: successful use forces immediate Crash Test (SR3 core p. 147).

---

### ALIENATION
*Type: M · Action: Complex · Range: LOS (or Zone) · Duration: Sustained*

Fills victims with profound estrangement and disconnection. Victims must make Willpower tests TN = critter's Essence to initiate any voluntary social contact or cooperative action. Zone variants affect all targets within the area rather than a single LOS target.

---

### ANIMAL CONTROL
*Type: M · Action: Complex · Range: LOS · Duration: Sustained*

Heightened empathy, usually limited to a specific animal type. Automatically prevents that animal type from attacking or raising alarms. By concentrating, the critter can control an individual animal, experiencing the world through its senses and directing behavior within what is normal for that animal (a controlled monkey cannot drive a car). Small animals (cats, rats): may control Charisma×5. Large animals (wolves, bears): may control Charisma.

---

### ASTRAL ARMOR
*Type: M · Action: Auto · Range: Self · Duration: Always*

Toughens astral form, providing armor equal to Essence. Astral Armor only protects fully astral beings; dual-natured creatures receive no benefit unless astrally projecting.

---

### BINDING
*Type: P · Action: Complex · Range: LOS or Touch · Duration: Instant*

Victim "sticks" to a surface or to the being itself. Binding Strength = twice critter's Essence. Victim must make Strength Test against Binding Strength to break free.

---

### BLINDNESS
*Type: M · Action: Complex · Range: LOS · Duration: Special*

Target is rendered effectively blind. Opposed test: critter's Essence vs target's Willpower. Duration and severity scale with net successes. Affects both natural and cybernetic vision unless noted otherwise.

---

### COLD AURA
*Type: P · Action: Free · Range: Self · Duration: Sustained*

Surface of the critter radiates intense cold. Adds +2 Power to melee attacks. Any successful melee attack against the critter forces the attacker to resist (critter's Essence)M cold damage. Can be extended to one victim within range on a Complex Action (see individual critter entry for range).

---

### COMPULSION
*Type: M · Action: Complex · Range: LOS (or Zone) · Duration: Sustained*

Forces a victim toward a specific type of behavior (fleeing, approaching, attacking, etc.) as specified per critter. Opposed test: critter Essence vs target Willpower. Net successes gauge severity and duration. Zone variants affect all in area.

---

### CONCEALMENT
*Type: P · Action: Simple · Range: LOS · Duration: Sustained*

Hides self or others within terrain. Adds critter's Essence to TN of any Perception Test to locate concealed subjects. Can conceal multiple targets simultaneously; concealed subjects can see each other. Directly opposed by Search: concealing critter's Essence adds to TN of searching critter's test.

---

### CONFUSION
*Type: M · Action: Complex · Range: LOS · Duration: Sustained*

Victims lose sense of direction and wander aimlessly. All Success Tests while under effect suffer a target modifier equal to critter's Essence. Whenever victim must make any decision, Willpower Test TN = critter's Essence; failure = unable to decide without external prompting (attack or verbal reminder). Unattended victim eventually wanders off.

---

### CONTROL RESISTANCE
*Type: M · Action: Auto · Range: Self · Duration: Always*

Critter is highly resistant to mind-affecting powers, spells, and magical control attempts. Adds critter's Essence to the TN of any attempt to magically control or influence it.

---

### CORROSIVE SALIVA
*Type: P · Action: Auto · Range: Touch · Duration: Instant*

Bite or spit attack carries highly corrosive acid. Damage Code as listed per critter. Can be used as ranged attack (spit) at range noted in entry. Standard damage resistance rules apply; some armor types may be ineffective against acid (GM discretion).

---

### CORROSIVE SECRETIONS
*Type: P · Action: Auto · Range: Touch · Duration: Instant*

Body secretions are corrosive. Any physical contact with the critter causes (Essence)M acid damage to the toucher. Affects weapons, gear, and unprotected flesh. Standard resistance tests apply.

---

### DARKNESS
*Type: P · Action: Complex · Range: LOS · Duration: Sustained*

Creates an area of magical darkness centered on or projected by the critter. All vision-based Perception Tests within the area suffer TN penalties. Low-light and thermographic vision are negated within the area. Radius typically equals critter's Essence in meters.

---

### DESIRE REFLECTION
*Type: M · Action: Complex · Range: LOS · Duration: Sustained*

Critter reads the target's deepest desires and reflects them back, creating a powerful compulsion. Target must resist with Willpower TN = critter's Essence. Failure causes the target to pursue or approach whatever they most desire, ignoring immediate threats.

---

### DISMISSAL
*Type: M · Action: Complex · Range: LOS · Duration: Instant*

Critter can dismiss summoned spirits. Treat as banishing attempt using critter's Essence as Magic Rating. Standard SR3 banishing rules apply (SR3 core p. 183).

---

### ELECTRICAL PROJECTION
*Type: P · Action: Complex · Range: LOS · Duration: Instant*

Critter discharges a powerful electrical attack. Damage Code as listed per critter. Standard electricity damage rules apply; conductive armor may be a liability (GM discretion). EMP variant (Lesser Thunderbird): affects electronics within range as an EMP burst rather than a direct damage attack.

---

### EMPATHY
*Type: M · Action: Complex · Range: LOS · Duration: Sustained*

Critter can sense and project emotions. Allows critter to understand the emotional state of any target within range and to project emotional states. Can be used to calm, agitate, or communicate simple concepts without language.

---

### ENGULF
*Type: P · Action: Exclusive Complex · Range: Touch · Duration: Sustained*

Draws victim into critter's body or element. Melee attack using critter's Quickness (not Reaction) to hit; target must counterattack successfully to avoid. Escape: Opposed Test, victim Strength vs critter Force/Essence each victim action.

- **Fire engulf:** (Force)M damage per critter action; +2 Power if critter also has Flame Aura; Impact armor resists, ballistic melts
- **Water engulf:** (Force)M Stun per critter action, +1 Power per action since engulfing began; Deadly Stun → Physical damage → drowning
- **Air engulf:** (Force)S Stun resisted by Willpower or Body (greater); breathing gear provides no protection; unconscious victim takes Physical damage → suffocation
- **Earth engulf:** (Force)S damage from crushing weight; Impact armor resists, ballistic does not

---

### ENHANCED MOVEMENT
*Type: P · Action: Auto · Range: Self · Duration: Always*

Critter moves at exceptional speed. Movement rate multiplied as noted in critter entry. In some entries this power allows reduction as well as increase (see Movement power for vehicle rules).

---

### ENHANCED PHYSICAL ATTRIBUTES
*Type: P · Action: Special · Range: Self · Duration: Special*

Critter can temporarily boost one or more physical Attributes. Format in entries: `EPA(Attribute ×N/day [E]Dt)` — N times per day, lasts [Essence]×dice turns. Multiple attributes may be boosted simultaneously if listed. Boosted stats affect all dependent derived stats (Reaction, damage codes, etc.) for the duration.

---

### ENHANCED REACTIONS
*Type: P · Action: Auto/Special · Range: Self · Duration: Special*

Critter has significantly elevated Reaction for test purposes. Format in entries: `ERct(×N/day [E]Dt)` — activated N times per day, lasts [Essence]×dice turns. While active, Reaction and Initiative reflect the enhanced value.

---

### ENHANCED SENSES
*Type: P · Action: Auto · Range: Self · Duration: Always*

Standard enhanced senses. All function as the equivalent cyberware/bioware versions for test and detection purposes. Common types:

| Abbreviation | Sense |
|---|---|
| LLV | Low-Light Vision |
| ThermoV | Thermographic Vision |
| Hear+ | Improved Hearing |
| Smell+ | Improved Smell |
| Taste+ | Improved Taste |
| Vision+ | Improved Vision / Depth Perception |
| Sonar | Sonar / Echolocation |
| MotionDet | Motion Detection (electrical field sensing) |
| Tactile | Enhanced Tactile Sense |
| AmpHear | Amplified Hearing |
| WideBandHear | Wide-Band Hearing |
| DepthPerc | Depth Perception |

---

### ESSENCE DRAIN
*Type: M · Action: Complex · Range: Touch · Duration: Instant*

Critter drains Essence from victims on physical contact or as noted per entry. Standard SR3 Essence Drain rules (SR3 core p. 129): temporary drain recovers; permanent drain does not.
- **Temporary (EsDrT):** Essence returns at standard recovery rate
- **Permanent (EsDrP):** Essence lost permanently; reduces Magic Rating if Awakened
- **Modified (EsDrM):** non-standard mechanic — see individual critter entry

---

### FADING
*Type: M · Action: Auto · Range: Self · Duration: Always*

Critter is difficult to perceive and track in astral space. Adds Essence to TN of any attempt to astrally perceive, track, or follow the critter.

---

### FEAR
*Type: M · Action: Complex · Range: LOS · Duration: Special*

Victim flees in panic toward nearest apparent safety. Opposed Test: victim's Willpower vs critter's Essence. Net successes determine severity. Terror lasts for Combat Turns equal to critter's net successes. Zone variant: affects all targets within area rather than single LOS target.

---

### FIRE RESISTANCE
*Type: P · Action: Auto · Range: Self · Duration: Always*

Functions as Immunity (Fire) at reduced effectiveness. Critter has an effective armor rating equal to Essence when resisting fire damage. Does not provide the full Immunity threshold protection; Power that exceeds Essence still deals damage (reduced by armor rating).

---

### FLAME AURA
*Type: P · Action: Free · Range: Self · Duration: Sustained*

Surface ripples with flame. Adds +2 Power to melee attacks. Any successful melee attack against the critter forces attacker to resist (critter Essence)M fire damage; Impact armor may help if armored portion struck. Intense variants may ignite wooden weapons or melt plastic/metal.

---

### FLAME PROJECTION
*Type: P · Action: Complex · Range: LOS · Duration: Instant*

Critter projects a directed stream or burst of fire. Damage Code as listed per critter. Standard fire damage rules apply. Range as listed; some critters project area-effect flames. Immunity to Fire does not protect from this if the Power exceeds twice the target's Essence.

---

### GLAMOUR
*Type: M · Action: Complex · Range: Zone · Duration: Sustained*

Critter creates powerful illusions over an area, masking its territory and disorienting intruders. Targets must make Resistance Tests (TN as listed in entry, typically [3×Essence]+2) to negate effect. Failed victims perceive false terrain, paths, or environmental features.

---

### GUARD
*Type: P · Action: Complex · Range: LOS · Duration: Sustained*

Prevents any accident — both natural and those caused by the Accident power — within terrain controlled by the critter. Also protects targets the critter designates from bad luck effects.

---

### HARDENED ARMOR
*Type: P · Action: Auto · Range: Self · Duration: Always*

Exoskeleton or shell of extraordinary strength. If attack Power (unmodified by burst fire or other modifiers) does not exceed armor rating, attack does no damage. Against APDS or AVM ammunition, Hardened Armor counts as half its rating only.

---

### HYPNOTIC SONG / HYPNOTIC DANCE
*Type: M · Action: Complex · Range: Zone · Duration: Sustained*

Audio or visual display that entrances victims. All targets within the zone (radius = critter Essence in meters unless noted) must make Willpower Tests TN = critter Essence or become entranced. Entranced victims stand motionless, unable to take actions, until the display stops or they take damage. Zone multiplier listed per critter (e.g., Zone×4 = radius 4×Essence meters). HypDance (Quicksilver Mongoose) functions identically but is visual rather than audio; effective only against snakes and creatures under 1m.

---

### ILLUSION
*Type: M · Action: Complex · Range: LOS · Duration: Sustained*

Critter creates convincing sensory illusions. Targets must make Opposed Test (Willpower vs critter Essence) to disbelieve. Failed targets treat illusions as real. Complexity of illusion varies by critter.

---

### IMMUNITY
*Type: P · Action: Auto · Range: Self · Duration: Always*

Critter has effective armor = twice Essence against the specified damage type. If attack Power does not exceed twice Essence, attack has no effect. Works against both magical and non-magical attacks.

- **Immunity to Age:** critter does not age; immune to aging effects
- **Immunity to Fire:** resists all fire-based attacks
- **Immunity to Normal Weapons:** no effect against Combat Spells or Weapon Foci; elemental damage halved (armor = Essence only); APDS/AVM treated as normal ammunition

---

### INFLUENCE
*Type: M · Action: Complex · Range: LOS · Duration: Instant*

Insinuates suggestions into victim's mind. Opposed Test: critter Charisma (or Essence if no Charisma) vs victim Willpower. Net successes gauge how strongly the suggestion takes effect. Can predispose toward action, reaction, or emotion.

Influence (Depression): victim experiences deep hopelessness and lethargy; Willpower Tests to take initiative or resist instruction suffer TN penalty = critter Essence.
Influence (Fear): as Fear power; victim flees.
Influence (Hopelessness): similar to Depression variant; GM discretion on mechanical expression.

---

### INNATE SPELL
*Type: P · Action: Complex · Range: LOS · Duration: Instant (or Sustained)*

Critter mimics a spell effect using Essence for both Sorcery and Force. Cannot be completely dodged with Combat Pool alone (unlike normal attacks). Awakened characters can use spell defense against it. Critter normally does not suffer Drain; if sustained, Drain Code = (Essence)S and effect spreads over Essence×Essence square meters. Sustained innate spells can be dispelled.

---

### INVISIBILITY (Non-Magical)
*Type: P · Action: Auto · Range: Self · Duration: Always*

Critter has natural non-magical transparency or camouflage so complete as to render it effectively invisible at rest or slow movement. All Perception Tests to spot the critter suffer a TN penalty equal to critter's Essence. Does not function against Ultrasound, Sonar, Thermal, or Magic Sense. Distinct from Adaptive Coloration (which is active) — this is always-on.

---

### MAGICAL GUARD
*Type: M · Action: Complex · Range: LOS · Duration: Sustained*

Acts as continuous Shielding metamagic for targets the critter designates. Adds critter Essence to dice pools for resisting any magical effect (spells, critter powers) for the protected target.

---

### MAGICAL RESISTANCE
*Type: M · Action: Auto · Range: Self · Duration: Always*

Innate resistance to magic. Adds Essence to TN of all magical attacks (Combat Spells, manipulation spells) directed at the critter. Does not apply to physical critter powers.

---

### MAGIC SENSE
*Type: M · Action: Auto · Range: Self · Duration: Always*

Critter can detect the presence of active magic, magical auras, and astral forms within range equal to Essence in meters without assensing. Does not provide full aura reading; detects presence only unless noted otherwise.

---

### MANIFESTATION
*Type: P · Action: Exclusive Simple · Range: Self · Duration: Special*

Primarily astral critter assumes solid physical form. Physical Attributes in material form = Essence unless noted. Materialized spirits: immune to age, pathogens, toxins; Immunity to Normal Weapons based on Essence; +10 Initiative; can physically attack using Reaction. Returning to astral: Exclusive Simple Action. Most nature spirits cannot remain materialized past sunset or sunrise.

---

### MASKING
*Type: M · Action: Auto · Range: Self · Duration: Always*

Critter's magical aura is masked or concealed from astral perception. Assensing tests against the critter suffer TN penalty = critter Essence. Astrally perceiving characters may not detect the critter's true nature or power level.

---

### MIMICRY
*Type: P · Action: Complex · Range: Self · Duration: Sustained*

Critter can replicate sounds, voices, or visual appearances. Audio mimicry: reproduces any sound the critter has previously heard, including speech. Visual mimicry: can alter appearance to resemble other creatures or objects (limited by body size). Perception Tests to identify the critter as false TN = critter Essence.

---

### MIST FORM
*Type: P · Action: Complex · Range: Self · Duration: Sustained*

Critter can transform its body into a diffuse mist or gaseous state. In mist form: immune to physical attacks; can pass through any opening not airtight; cannot make physical attacks; can still use mana-based powers; movement rate as listed. Resuming solid form requires a Complex Action.

---

### MOVEMENT
*Type: P · Action: Complex · Range: LOS · Duration: Sustained*

Increases or decreases target's movement rate by multiplying or dividing by critter's Essence. Against vehicles: Essence Test TN = half vehicle's Body; successes × Acceleration Rating added or subtracted from Speed each Combat Turn (similar to vehicle acceleration/deceleration test); may trigger Crash or Stress Test. Movement (Decrease) variant applies only the reduction effect.

---

### NOXIOUS BREATH
*Type: P · Action: Complex · Range: Touch · Duration: Instant*

Projects nauseating stench. Victim resists with Willpower or Body (greater) against Damage Code (Essence)S Stun. Armor and dermal plating provide no protection. Respirator reduces Power by 2 and stages damage down one level.

---

### PARALYZING HOWL
*Type: M · Action: Complex · Range: Zone · Duration: Special*

Sound-based paralysis effect. All targets in zone make Willpower Test TN = critter Essence. Failure = paralyzed for Combat Turns equal to critter net successes. Zone radius = critter Essence in meters unless noted.

---

### PARALYZING TOUCH
*Type: M · Action: Complex · Range: Touch · Duration: Special*

Contact paralysis. Target makes Willpower or Body Test (greater) TN = critter Essence. Failure = paralyzed. Duration and recovery as per standard SR3 paralysis rules.

---

### PETRIFICATION
*Type: P · Action: Complex · Range: Touch · Duration: Permanent*

Target's flesh slowly turns to stone over the course of one Combat Turn. Opposed Test: critter Essence vs target Body. Net successes determine how much of the target is affected. Full failure = complete petrification, effectively dead.

---

### PETRIFYING GAZE
*Type: M · Action: Complex · Range: LOS · Duration: Permanent*

As Petrification but range is LOS. Target must meet the critter's gaze (Perception Test TN = critter Essence to avoid looking). Once eye contact is made, Opposed Test applies as above.

---

### PESTILENCE
*Type: P · Action: Auto · Range: Touch · Duration: Special*

Critter carries or generates dangerous pathogens. Any contact with the critter or its attacks may transmit disease. GM determines specific disease parameters; treat as standard SR3 disease/toxin rules. Some critters: roll 1D6 per individual to determine if that specific animal has the power (see entry).

---

### PSYCHOKINESIS
*Type: P · Action: Complex · Range: LOS · Duration: Sustained*

Generates psychokinetic energy with Strength and Quickness equal to critter's Essence, functioning as the Magic Fingers spell (SR3 core p. 197). Can manipulate objects at range.

---

### REGENERATION
*Type: P · Action: Auto · Range: Self · Duration: Always*

Critter heals at an accelerated rate. Heals one box of Physical damage per Combat Turn (or as specified per critter entry). Wounds inflicted by the critter's listed vulnerability substance do not regenerate any faster than normal healing.

---

### SEARCH
*Type: P · Action: Exclusive Complex · Range: LOS · Duration: Special*

Locates any person, place, or object within critter's terrain. Opposed Test: critter (2×Essence) vs target's Intelligence. Against objects: critter (2×Essence) vs Object Resistance (SR3 core p. 182). Concealment directly opposes Search: concealing critter's Essence adds to TN of Search test.

---

### SILENCE
*Type: P · Action: Complex · Range: Zone · Duration: Sustained*

Creates a zone of magical or natural silence. All sound-based Perception Tests within zone fail automatically. Zone radius = critter Essence in meters unless noted. Non-Magical Silence variant (Gloaming Owl, Shadowhound): functions identically but cannot be dispelled by mages.

---

### SONIC PROJECTION
*Type: P · Action: Complex · Range: LOS · Duration: Instant*

Critter projects a focused or area burst of damaging sound. Damage Code as listed. Standard Damage Resistance applies; hearing protection may reduce Power (GM discretion). High-Frequency variant (Pricuricu): affects electronics and targets with cybernetic hearing enhancement specifically.

---

### SPRAYING
*Type: P · Action: Complex · Range: Touch (Special) · Duration: Instant*

Critter sprays a substance (musk, acid, venom) in a cone or stream. Range = ¼ normal unless noted otherwise. Standard Damage Resistance applies. Some spray attacks have secondary effects (blindness, stench, paralysis) — see individual entry.

---

### VENOM
*Type: P · Action: Auto · Range: Touch · Duration: Instant*

Bite or sting is venomous. Damage Code = (Essence)S. Treat as toxin with Speed 1 turn. Standard SR3 toxin resistance rules apply (SR3 core p. 124). Some critters have special secondary venom effects — see individual entries.

---

### WEATHER CONTROL
*Type: P · Action: Complex · Range: LOS · Duration: Sustained*

Critter can influence local weather conditions. Effects scale with Essence: minor effects (shifting wind, light rain) at low Essence; severe effects (storms, lightning, blizzards) at high Essence. Electrical Storms variant (Lesser Thunderbird): specifically generates lightning strike conditions. Any variant (Man-of-the-Woods): can produce any weather effect.

## Weakness Rules

### ALLERGY
Critter's metabolism is disrupted by a specific substance. Severity levels:

| Severity | Passive (contact) | Active (ingestion/immersion) |
|---|---|---|
| Nuisance | −1 die pool | Minor discomfort |
| Mild | −2 die pool; minor damage | Moderate reaction |
| Moderate | Significant penalties | Serious damage |
| Severe | Major damage and penalties | Dangerous |
| Extreme | Potentially fatal | Life-threatening |

### VULNERABILITY
Wounds inflicted by the specified substance have their Damage Level increased by 1 (e.g., 2L becomes 2M). Critter recovers from vulnerability wounds at normal recovery rate even if it has Regeneration.

### DIETARY REQUIREMENT
Critter must regularly consume the specified substance or suffer progressive deterioration. GM determines frequency and effects of deprivation.

### ESSENCE LOSS
Critter's Essence decays over time. Rate specified per entry. At Essence 0, critter dies or ceases to function. Some critters can compensate by draining Essence from victims.

### INDUCED DORMANCY
Critter is rendered comatose by specific conditions (e.g., lack of air for vampires). Duration = until condition is removed.

### REDUCED SENSES
Critter has impaired or absent sensory capability as noted. Standard SR3 penalties for the relevant sensory deficit apply.

## Dice Pools (Spirits and Dragons)

**Combat Pool:** (Quickness + Intelligence + Willpower) ÷ 2, rounded down. Standard combat pool rules apply. Dual-natured beings use Combat Pool even in astral combat (unless astrally projecting).

**Astral Combat Pool:** Astrally projecting dragons use (Intelligence + Charisma + Willpower) ÷ 2. Spirits: Force × 1.5. Standard astral combat rules apply.

**Spell Pool (dragons with Sorcery only):** (Intelligence + Willpower + Essence) ÷ 3, rounded down.

**Karma Pool:** GM-determined. As a guideline, base on average of player characters' Karma Pools adjusted for threat level. Dragon Karma Pool should be at least double the player average.

---

## Initiative (Spirits and Dragons)

Base: Reaction + Initiative dice roll. Materialized spirits: +10 Initiative. Astrally projecting: +20 Initiative, same number of Initiative dice as physical. Dragon astral combat: use normal physical Initiative.
