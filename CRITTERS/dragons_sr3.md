## Dragon Rules

**Communication:** Dragons cannot produce verbal speech. They communicate telepathically with beings in line of sight. Telepathic communication cannot be intercepted by technical devices. Dragons dealing with technology sometimes use metahuman translators.

**Astrally active:** All dragons have Essence (Z) notation — dual natured, exist simultaneously in physical and astral space.

**Creative license:** Unlike standard critters, dragons are treated as important NPCs with full individual variation in skills, magic, powers, and personality. Stat blocks are baselines — individual dragons exceed or fall short in ways appropriate to their age, history, and nature. The older and greater the dragon, the more complexity and power is appropriate.

**Great Dragons:** All great dragon Attributes marked (+) are added to the base stats of their dragon type. Great dragons are all magicians of significant power. Some can shapechange into human or metahuman form (exact prevalence unknown).

**Shape Change (Great Dragons):** Attributes in parentheses apply in human/metahuman form where listed. Human form stats follow individual entry.

## Known Great Dragons

*Campaign-relevant notes. Dragons are not fully statted here — treat as GM-controlled forces of nature with resources, goals, and capabilities that exceed what any stat block can represent.*

**Aden** — Sirrush. Demolished Tehran in 2020 after the ruling ayatollah declared jihad against the Awakened. Believed to lair atop Mount Ararat.

**Alamais** — Western. Terrorist; believed involved with Der Nachtmachen, Alt Welt, and other Germano-European policlubs. Present whereabouts unknown.

**Celedyr** — Western. Lairs in Caerleon, Britain, beneath an ancient Roman amphitheater that is the center of a Transys Neuronet corporate research installation. Seen only three times since the Awakening.

**Dunkelzahn** — Western. Primary source of public information about dragon society. First non-homo sapiens to run for elected office (2057 UCAS presidential race). Assassinated shortly after taking office by a mysterious explosion outside the Watergate Hotel in the Federal District of Columbia. Could shapechange into human form.

**Hestaby** — Western. Lairs atop Mount Shasta in the California Free State. Maintains a small community of humans and metahumans. Repelled a Tir Tairngire invasion of Northern California; prime force holding Tir Tairngire at bay in the Pacific Northwest.

**Hualpa** — Feathered Serpent. Leader and spokesbeing of the Awakened forces of Amazonia. Believed to lair in the Yucatan.

**Lofwyr** — Western. CEO and Chairdragon of Saeder-Krupp Heavy Industries — largest and most powerful corporation in the world. Member of the Council of Princes of Tir Tairngire. Lairs in the Rhine-Ruhr Megaplex, German Alliance.

**Lung** — Eastern. Involved in multiple Triad wars post-Awakening, sponsoring factions with monetary and magical support. Current whereabouts unknown; believed to lair somewhere in mainland China.

**Mujaji** — "Rain Queen." Feathered Serpent. Lairs in the Cape Republic, Africa. Antagonism noted with neighboring elves of the Zulu Nation.

**Rhonabwy** — Western. Lairs in Llandovery, Wales. Corporate power-player; loves fine music; strong supporter of metahuman rights. May have a great dragon enemy known only as "The Sea Dragon" in Caradigan Bay (may be local legend).

**Ryumyo** — Eastern. First dragon seen by humans — revealed himself December 24, 2011 near Mount Fuji; two further sightings at Ise and Kyoto, then vanished. Rumored ties to Yakuza in Japan.

**Sirrurg** — Western. Believed responsible for loss of EuroAir Flight 329 in 2041. Identified in several attacks against corporate and government holdings in Europe; may have participated in the coup establishing Amazonia. Present whereabouts unknown.

### PERIANWYR *(Western Dragon — Middle-Aged)*
*Source: SR1 — see SR3 reconciliation notes*

Awakened in 2022 from a centuries-long sleep in a Welsh cavern. Emerged ravenous and ate a flock of sheep; a mob of angry farmers nearly killed him. A local boy named Kyle Morgan held them off with a shotgun and sheer charisma. Perianwyr has worked the shadows with Morgan ever since — 27+ years as of campaign date. Morgan is his only friend; they share an empathic link allowing each to feel the other's emotions. Despite fierce loyalty to Morgan, Perianwyr is apathetic about the current mission and wants to end their Aztechnology employment. Any attack on Morgan triggers merciless retaliation.

`B15/8 Q6×3 S40 C4 I5 W8 E8Z R5 | Init: 5+2d6 | Atk: 14D +2Reach`

**SR3 reconciliation — SR1 source:**
- Attack `14D` = SR1 Deadly code; SR3 equivalent is `14D` with standard SR3 staging (every 2 net successes stages up/down). No staging number was explicit in SR1.
- Threat/Professional Rating 6/4 = SR1 mechanic; no SR3 equivalent. Informational only — treat as indicating a serious, experienced combatant.
- Magic 8 listed separately from Essence 8 — in SR3, Magic Rating = Essence for spellcasting. Both align here; no conflict.
- Essence listed without parenthetical in source — dual natured applied (Z) consistent with dragon type.
- Dice pools from SR1 source omitted; recalculate using SR3 formula: Combat Pool = (Q+I+W)÷2 = (6+5+8)÷2 = **9**; Astral Combat Pool (projecting) = (I+C+W)÷2 = (5+4+8)÷2 = **8**.

## SR3 Dragon Mechanics Quick Reference

**Astral:** All dragons are dual natured (Z notation). Dual-natured beings use Combat Pool in astral combat unless astrally projecting.

**Initiative:** Base Reaction + Init dice roll. Standard dragon Init dice: 2D6. Great Dragon modifier: +1D6 over base type.

**Combat Pool:** (Quickness + Intelligence + Willpower) ÷ 2, rounded down.

**Astral Combat Pool (projecting):** (Intelligence + Charisma + Willpower) ÷ 2, rounded down.

**Spell Pool (Sorcery):** (Intelligence + Willpower + Essence) ÷ 3, rounded down.

**Karma Pool:** GM-determined. Minimum: double the average player Karma Pool for great dragons.

**Hardened Armor:** If attack Power (unmodified) does not exceed armor rating, attack does no damage. Against APDS/AVM: half rating only.

**Astral Armor:** Provides armor equal to Essence on the astral plane only. Dual-natured creatures receive no benefit unless astrally projecting.

**Telepathy:** LOS only. Cannot be intercepted by technical devices.

**Source edition notes:**

*SR1 source (Perianwyr):*
- Attack codes `14D` = SR1 Deadly level; no explicit staging. SR3: treat as `14D` with standard SR3 staging.
- Threat/Professional Rating has no SR3 equivalent — informational only.
- Dice pools not carried forward; recalculate from SR3 formula.

*SR2 source (Geyswain, Eliohann, Arleesh):*
- Attack `10D3` = SR2 notation: Damage Code 10, Deadly level, staging 3. SR3 equivalent: `10D` with staging 3 (every 3 net successes = one stage). GM discretion on whether to retain explicit staging or use SR3 default.
- Defense pool labels (Armed/Unarmed) map to SR3 Combat Pool; source values are baselines — recalculate from SR3 formula if needed.
- SR2 "Astral pool" maps to SR3 Astral Combat Pool formula above.
- Magic listed separately from Essence in SR2 — in SR3, Magic = Essence. Where they diverge, Essence takes precedence for spellcasting.
