## Downtown Hub — King Street / Union Station

Rail connections at King Street:

| Line | Direction | Security at Terminal | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| South Corridor | Auburn / Tacoma / Lakewood | A | Primary campaign route |
| North Corridor | Tukwila / Everett | A | Boeing worker traffic |
| Eastside Line | Bellevue / Renton / Kirkland | A | Lake Washington crossing; Bellevue terminus is tighter |

Other connections: Monorail (nearby), Bus grid (multiple routes), Waterfront Ferry Terminal (10–15 min surface transit).

SIN exposure: Functional gates, permanent LS detail (4–6 visible). Peak hours = high volume, lower individual scrutiny. Off-peak = same officers, far fewer people, higher scrutiny. Credstick passes available at kiosks for nuyen — logged but inconsistently enforced.
