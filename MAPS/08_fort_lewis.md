## Jurisdiction *(2060)*
Fort Lewis is patrolled by three overlapping authorities whose jurisdictions are in dispute:
- **Lone Star Security Services**
- **Metroplex Guard**
- **UCAS Military Police**

The Metroplex Guard and UCAS Military Police consider each area to be at a security rating **one level higher** than Lone Star's rating.
