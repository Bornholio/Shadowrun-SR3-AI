## Volcanic Zone Notes

`Orting` — Z-rated. Sits in the primary Rainier lahar path. No maintained transit. Foot or improvised vehicle only.

`Carbanado` — E-rated (Puyallup district). Carbon River lahar corridor. SR3 name for Carbonado. No transit access. Seoulpa Ring presence noted.

`Sumner confluence` — Most geologically exposed functional station on the south line. White River and Puyallup River lahar paths both converge here. Infrastructure shows it.

`Chokepoint rule` — White River and Puyallup River crossings are the natural containment points for the Auburn–Sumner corridor. Minimal bridging options; easy to seal.

`Glow City` — Z-rated radioactive zone, Redmond. No transit. Contamination gradient precedes the hard boundary. SR3 has no detailed rad mechanic — GM discretion on exposure damage. Fringe used by scavengers and criminal operations only.
