## Genetic Heritage Overview

All seven carry latent elven or Night One genetics from pre-Awakening lineages.
Expression was not passive — it required contact with early magical events of the 6th World.
The Great Ghost Dance (2017) was the primary adult expression trigger.
Carpenter's doubled reinforcement crossed the birth-expression threshold — UGE baby, 2011.

## Keystone

### Genetic Profile
Father is Irish. Genetic base is Norwegian — Viking settlement lineage maintained
in the Irish population, which carries a disproportionately high concentration
of elven genetics in the SR3 timeline. The Norwegian elven strain persisted
through the Irish branch, not diluted by it.
Strong 4th World characteristics. Albinism carried in the lineage.

### Early Life
Born Florida. Raised on a farm in North Dakota.
Large Norwegian extended family. Father absent.
Did not finish college. Drafted into the army.
Went AWOL during AIT.
Met Kluger's mother during that period. FBI pursuit active.
Kluger born from this relationship — Keystone unaware.
Fled to Canada.

### Vancouver Years
Met and married Meridian in Vancouver, BC.
Singer born in Vancouver.
Hippie household. Moved frequently.
Keystone worked for the railroad. Meridian cooked.

### Los Angeles
Carter amnesty (1977) brought the family — now including Crowbar — to Los Angeles.
Meridian managed restaurants.
Keystone attended mechanics school. Ran a lawn care business.

### North Dakota — Return
Mid-1980s: entire family relocated to a small town in North Dakota.
Meridian cooking. Keystone running an auto mechanic shop.

### Career Peak
Returned to school — completed civil engineering degree.
Brief period in used car sales.
Joined the state bridge office. Rose to lead design teams.
Eventually headed the state bridge division.

### Expression and Retirement
Retired in the wake of the Great Ghost Dance and elven expression (2017).
The professional world he built a career in no longer existed in the same form.

### Kluger Thread — GM Controlled
Discovered Kluger's existence in the early 1980s.
Attempted contact. Refused by Kluger's maternal family. Did not pursue further.
Carries this without having raised it with Meridian or anyone else.
Whether Meridian knows: GM controlled.
Kluger subsequently made the decision as an adult to seek contact.
Whether contact was established before the reset: GM controlled.

### Dag
Named Dag approximately 40 years before the reset — Seattle area,
likely during an Alaska cruise stopover — without conscious intent.
The bond is visible in astral space as a settled thread, old and rooted.

### Hobbies
Contemporary jazz (1980–2020 era — specific to that window)
Jazz guitar — plays, has modified his own instruments
Guitar collection and modification
Speaker design
Furniture building
*Hands-on making is the through-line — engineering expressed personally.*

## Meridian

### Genetic Profile
Czech father — genetic albino Night One, full expression carrier.
British mother — partial Night One, half carrier only.
75% Night One / 25% elf expression.
Expression draws heavily from the Czech paternal line.

### Early Life
Born in the UK during WWII.
Father: Czech SIS agent — sent behind enemy lines by British intelligence.
Mother: French language instructor. British national.
Early teen years spent in Czechoslovakia — paternal family context.

### Language Note — GM Only
Meridian claims not to know Czech. Speaks it fine.
This is a habit from the KGB threat period — operational cover maintained
so long it feels like truth. The family post-reset has no reason to know she speaks it.
Speaks Parisian French fluently — register is now dated, pre-1960s academic.
This is the "1960s academic register" noted in campaign_stats.md.

### Flight from Prague
Family fled the Russian invasion of Prague (1968).
Czech KGB assassination attempts followed — father's SIS history made the family a target.
UK government response: forced/sponsored relocation to Canada.
This is how Meridian ends up in Vancouver — not by choice, by managed extraction.

### Vancouver and Family
Met Keystone in Vancouver following the family's relocation.
Married Keystone. Singer born in Vancouver.
Cooked through the railroad years. Managed restaurants in Los Angeles.

### Career
Returned to school — occupational therapy degree (late 1980s/early 1990s).
Traveling occupational therapist through the 1990s and into the 2000s.
Ranged more widely as the therapy market contracted.
Retired approximately one year before Keystone, ahead of the expression wave.

### Hobbies
Antiques
Museums
Travel
Karaoke
Flowers
