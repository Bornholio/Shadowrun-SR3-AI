## The Family

Seven elves. A family unit — by blood, by choice, by history.
They have known each other across decades. The connections run deep
even when the memories do not.

## Keystone

**Born:** Florida
**Raised:** North Dakota farm — large Norwegian extended family

Started college, did not finish. Drafted into the army, went AWOL during training.
Fled to Canada.

Met and married Meridian in Vancouver, BC. Singer born there.
Hippie household. Moved frequently. Worked for the railroad.

Carter amnesty (1977) brought the family to Los Angeles.
Attended mechanics school. Ran a lawn care business.

Mid-1980s: family relocated to a small town in North Dakota.
Ran an auto mechanic shop.

Returned to school — completed a civil engineering degree.
Brief period in used car sales. Joined the state bridge office.
Rose through the ranks to head the state bridge division.

Retired in the wake of the Great Ghost Dance and elven expression (2017).

**Hobbies:** Contemporary jazz (1980–2020), jazz guitar, guitar building and modification,
speaker design, furniture building

---

## Meridian

**Born:** United Kingdom, WWII era
**Background:** British mother, Czech father

Early teen years spent in Czechoslovakia.
Family fled to Canada following political upheaval in Prague (1968).
Settled in Vancouver.

Met and married Keystone in Vancouver.
Cooked through the early years. Managed restaurants in Los Angeles.

Returned to school — occupational therapy degree.
Traveling occupational therapist through the 1990s and 2000s.
Retired approximately one year before Keystone.

**Languages:** English, French (Parisian — pre-1960s register), Sperethiel
**Hobbies:** Antiques, museums, travel, karaoke, flowers

---

## Singer

**Born:** Vancouver, BC — to Keystone and Meridian

Left a computer science degree mid-program to join the USAF.
Served as a military electronics warfare specialist.

Post-service: electronics technician. Ran a small circuit fabrication operation.
Returned to school — mechanical engineering degree.
Worked in high energy physics.
Moved to a science company designing small robots and chemical analysis equipment.
Later ran industrial robot lines for a large manufacturer,
then designed and built robot work cells.
Laid off 2018 following post-expression economic disruption.

Met Banshee at the end of his service tour.

**Hobbies:** Linguistics, reading and speed reading, science fiction,
cryptography, pre-Awakening magic theory

---

## Banshee

**Background:** Northern and central European heritage

Spokane to Portland and back. Met Singer at the end of his service tour.

State government work through the 1990s — IRS and medical sectors.
Occupational therapist assistant degree, end of the decade.
Followed with communications and teaching degrees,
specializing in special education.
Worked in public education. Para-educator during the disruption years.
Ended the 2000s teaching police officers.

Sign Language 5 — state-level deaf and hard-of-hearing advocate.

**Hobbies:** Reading (300+ books per year), sign language, romance novels,
arts and crafts, painting

---

## Carpenter

**Born:** 2011 — UGE wave, expressed as elf from birth
**Parents:** Singer and Banshee

Born with skull and bone irregularities — resolved surgically in infancy.
Youngest of the seven by decades.
Prior life: student. Still forming when the memories end.

**Hobbies:** Legos, carpentry and woodworking, outdoorsmanship, gold mining

---

## Crowbar

**Background:** Part of the family unit since the Los Angeles years (1977 onward)

Escalating legal trouble through and after high school.
Traveled the country doing odd jobs.
Caught returning from BC. Served a short jail term.
In and out of trouble through the 2010s.

**Hobbies:** Tattooing, drawing and sketching, guitar, bicycle repair,
reggae and counterculture music

---

## Kluger

**Background:** Italian heritage. Teenage years in Europe — Germany specifically.

Returned to Florida. Later relocated to St. Louis.
Medical service provider through the 1990s.
Transitioned to in-home nursing and special needs care into the 2000s.
Relocated in the 2010s to be close to family.

**Hobbies:** Reading, folk music, European history, animals and zoos

## Shared Touchstones

- All seven were in the vicinity of the Great Ghost Dance in 2017.
  This is when elven expression occurred for six of the seven.
  Carpenter was already expressed from birth.
- All seven remember clearly up to approximately 2025.
  What happened between 2025 and waking at McHugh's in 2058
  is the question none of them can yet answer.
