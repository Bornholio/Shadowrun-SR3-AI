## Team Generson Notes

Human masked cover identity. Composition: Rigger, Decker, Two Sams (Samurai).
Ran from at least 09/23/50 through 03/06/55 — five years of documented use.
May still exist in world records. NPCs who knew Team Generson will not automatically connect them to current Night One elves.
Load on demand when Team Generson is referenced in play.

## Herbertson Medical Services

Corporation established 11/25/51. Physical HQ: Taetzel building, 28-story former abandoned office building, Redmond Barrens.
Taetzel building subsequently sold — no longer family asset.
Corporate filings, contracts, and paper trail exist in the world whether the family remembers them or not.
Load on demand when corporate history becomes relevant.

## Pentarus

Elven initiate, hermetic mage/decker, moderately augmented.
Led the group through all prior adventures as primary tactician and early face.
Face role later transitioned to Banshee.
Killed shortly after Super Tuesday — circumstances GM controlled.
Possible connection to investigating the president's assassination.
Extensive contact list exists — provides partial context to Banshee.
Details withheld to avoid red herrings. Load on demand.

## The Reset — Why It Happened

The family maintained at operational peak:
- Hundreds of contacts
- Multiple lifestyle covers and SIN identities
- Herbertson Medical Services corporation with physical infrastructure
- Ongoing community obligations (1 GK/week sustained)
- Three prime runners with extraordinary augmentation loads

Carrying all of that forward conscious was determined to be untenable or dangerous.
Someone — or the family themselves — chose the reset.
The family does not know they chose it.
