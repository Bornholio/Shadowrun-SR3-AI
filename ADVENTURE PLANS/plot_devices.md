## THE ATTACK AND PUBLIC DEATH

### Date
August 11, 2057 — with final confirmed activity August 19, 2057.

### Event
The family was subjected to a combined, explosive, magical, and toxin attack. The attack was sufficiently public and decisive that all persons outside the 14 current contacts believe the family dead. This is not a rumor — it is an established fact in the world.

### The Public Death
The attack constituted a public death event. Burials occurred seven days later. The funerals were extremely public with many attendees. The scale and visibility of the burial was deliberate — ensuring that the world accepted the death as real and final.

### Immediate Aftermath
At the time of the attack and immediately following, Harlequin had the family moved to trauma facilities. He is neutral to the family — he owes them for assistance rendered during metaplane operations — and does not act against them. This is the full extent of his obligation as it applies to this event: he ensured they survived and reached medical care.

### Harlequin's Role
Harlequin agreed to coordinate the medical recovery planning. His involvement is limited to this agreement and to the work of his agent. He does not directly manage the reconstruction. He is not present during the reconstruction period in any confirmed capacity. His knowledge of the family is complete — he knows their true pre-reset identities. This knowledge is held by him and his Free Spirit agent Ariel only.

## THE RECONSTRUCTION PERIOD

### Timeline
Approximately six months total, beginning in late August/early September 1957 and concluding in late February/early March 2058. The longest single procedure — genetic recovery — ran continuously throughout the entire reconstruction period. All other work was staged around the genetic timeline.

### Awareness States
The family passed through various states of awareness during reconstruction. In the final weeks before full emergence, they were awake enough to make decisions. The specific decisions made during this window — what they asked for, what they consented to, what they chose — are GM controlled. The HMS chemicals that arrested the Laés damage were developed by the family themselves at HMS prior to the attack, suggesting either foreknowledge of the Laés threat or precautionary preparation against magical memory disruption in general.

### Facilities Involved

**PensoDyne — Yamatetsu Research Institute**
Primary facility. Vat reconstruction, genetic work, primary healing ritual space. The most complete technical record of the reconstruction exists here, or existed here — a PensoDyne high-level manager, fearing massive liquidated damages exposure from the contract, began planning a data dump and full erasure of all records as the vat work neared completion. Whether this dump was fully executed, partially executed, or prevented is GM controlled. If any PensoDyne records survived, they represent the most complete external technical documentation of the reconstruction. PensoDyne's involvement connects the family's reconstruction to Yamatetsu corporate infrastructure.

**HMS Facility — Herbertson Medical Services**
The family's own corporate infrastructure was used for delta-grade cyberware installation. HMS was sufficient to handle this work. This means the family's corporation was actively involved in their own reconstruction — either by pre-arrangement or by activation during the recovery window when they were awake enough to make decisions. The HMS billing account HMS-7731-CORP remains active as of session start, which means HMS infrastructure was never fully shut down after the reconstruction.

**Shiawase Subsidiary**
Grew all cultured bioware implants. The subsidiary was paid a significant bonus to destroy all plans and samples upon completion of the work. Destruction is assumed but not canonically confirmed. If any Shiawase subsidiary records survived, they would document the bioware package in detail. The identity of the specific subsidiary is GM controlled.

**Yamatetsu — Nanotech**
Primary source for nanotech implants. Yamatetsu's dual role — hosting PensoDyne and supplying nanotech — means Yamatetsu corporate has deeper involvement in the reconstruction than any other single corporation.

**Cross Applied Technologies — Guardian Angel System**
The Guardian Angel nanotech system came from CAT, not Yamatetsu. This is the sole exception to the Yamatetsu nanotech sourcing. CAT records of the sale may exist. GM controlled whether traceable.

### Persons Present During Reconstruction

**Ariel — Free Spirit, Harlequin's Agent**
Handled the operational legwork of the reconstruction. Located the advanced genetic and bioware facilities. Coordinated between the various corporate entities. Is aware of the family's masked auras — the genetic restructuring made their DNA and auras unrecognizable, and Ariel was present throughout this transformation. Knows the family's current form but not their pre-reset identities — her involvement began during reconstruction after the genetic work was underway. Does not know their pre-reset identities. GM controlled whether she appears in active play.

**Jane Foster**
Does some of the work during reconstruction. Assumes the work she is doing is for others — her full knowledge of the situation is limited. Identity and current status GM controlled.

**Outside Personnel — Facilities Staff**
Various corporate personnel at PensoDyne, Shiawase subsidiary, Yamatetsu, HMS were involved in the physical work. These individuals knew the family only as heavily mangled elves. The first work performed was genetic — meaning that even their DNA and aura became unrecognizable very early in the reconstruction process. By the time most facility personnel interacted with them in any sustained way, the family's identity was already unreachable through physical or magical examination. No facility personnel can connect the family to their pre-reset identities.

**Aldric**
Participated in continuous long-term ritual magic healing during the reconstruction period. This is the confirmed window in which he met Flurry, who was the only creature present throughout the entire reconstruction. His participation was likely at PensoDyne where the ritual space existed. He knows the family as they became during reconstruction — not as they were before. He knows each of the seven individually from months of ritual proximity. He knows their auras in their current form. He was present for the Laés event and its aftermath. He knows the memories are potentially recoverable. He does not know their pre-reset identities — the genetic restructuring preceded his involvement, their auras were already unrecognizable when he arrived.

**Flurry — Blackberry Cat**
The only creature present continuously from attack through reconstruction through waking. Dual-natured — she has been astrally observing everything since before the family woke. She recognized Aldric immediately at their first physical meeting in Session 12. She is the living continuity thread that the family does not yet know they have. She cannot speak but she observes and remembers. She was with the family when they were at their most vulnerable and she is with them now.

### What Was Liquidated
Ariel liquidated all family assets during the reconstruction period. Everything was sold. The exceptions were a small number of packs the family specifically asked to be prepared — GM controlled what these contain. The Taetzel building (Herbertson Medical Services physical HQ, 28-story Redmond Barrens) was sold. Team Generson cover identity assets were dissolved. The extensive prior contact network was not maintained — those contacts still exist in the world but have no current relationship with the family.

## THE LAÉS EVENT

### The Drug
Laés was developed by the government of Tir Tairngire. It erases short-term memories in a retrograde fashion, beginning from the moment of injection and flowing backward through the subject's memories. Subjects fall unconscious on administration. Laés is used by the Tir to memory-wipe border infiltrators, who are then dumped outside the nation. It is normally irreversible — there is no published antidote or recovery protocol.

### When It Was Administered
In the final week or so before the vat work was complete. The family was at or near the end of reconstruction — the genetic work finalizing, the augmentation packages installed or nearly so. They were administered a massive dose.

### The Tir Agent
A Tir Tairngire government agent administered the Laés. The agent failed to exit the PensoDyne facility afterward. HMS security agents killed the Tir agent on site. The agent's identity, their chain of command, and who authorized the operation are GM controlled. The Tir's interest in erasing the family's memories of the reconstruction period — or erasing the memories of the reconstruction itself — connects directly to the three Tir data points already established in the campaign:

1. Ciarán Ó Maolalaidh — HMS signatory with Tir forwarding address, GT-protected
2. Trinity nightclub shell license — Tir Tairngire registration
3. Tamanous pipeline shell account ownership — Tir Tairngire registration, three layers deep

The Alachia thread is sealed. Surface at GM pace only. The Tir's interest in the family is not coincidental and predates the reset.

### Effect of the Laés
Retrograde amnesia flowing backward from the injection moment. A massive dose means the regression went deep — potentially all the way back to 2025, which is the current cutoff of the family's accessible memories. The Laés did not stop on its own.

### The HMS Chemical Intervention
The family had previously developed specialized chemicals at HMS that were capable of arresting Laés damage. These chemicals are not a published formulation — they are proprietary HMS development. They were available and were administered after the Laés, stopping the retrograde regression at some point. The result is that the family's memory loss is not a clean irreversible wipe — it is an arrested regression with a defined cutoff. Memory is potentially recoverable.

The foreknowledge implied by the existence of these chemicals is significant. The family developed a Laés antidote-adjacent compound before they needed it. Either they anticipated the specific Laés threat, or they developed precautionary anti-amnesia compounds as a general operational measure given their prior career. Either interpretation has implications for what the family knew before the attack.

### The Memory Architecture — Layered Disruption
The family's memory loss is the result of two distinct events operating together:

**Layer 1 — The Reset (chosen)**
The family, during the window when they were awake enough to make decisions, chose some form of reset. The scale of what they had been maintaining — hundreds of contacts, multiple lifestyle covers, HMS corporation, ongoing community obligations, three prime runners with extraordinary augmentation loads — made carrying all of that forward conscious untenable or dangerous. Someone, or the family themselves, determined that a clean emergence was necessary. This choice shaped what was preserved and what was released.

**Layer 2 — The Laés (imposed)**
The Tir agent administered Laés to erase the reconstruction period specifically. The HMS chemicals arrested this regression. The interaction between the chosen reset and the Laés means the current memory cutoff at 2025 may not be purely a result of choice — the Laés regression may have pushed further back than intended, and the chemicals stopped it where they stopped it.

**The Implication**
The family may believe they chose to forget everything after 2025. The reality may be that they chose to forget the reconstruction and recent operational period, and the Laés pushed the regression further than intended. The HMS chemicals stopped the damage but could not reverse what had already been erased. What they chose to forget and what the Laés took may not be identical. Aldric knows this. This is almost certainly part of what he is afraid someone else will explain first.

## THE ORGANOMETALLIC KEY

### Physical Description
Approximately 8-10 centimeters long. Shaft and head carved to resemble a tree. Parts of the elaborate carvings and filigree are worn smooth with age and handling. Surface holds a dull shine. Resembles ancient mechanical keys common two centuries ago. Virtually indestructible — no physical or magical force can scratch it. This is real 4th World magic at work.

### Material
Orichalcum — the canonical 4th World magical metal. The campaign description of "organometallic, partially grown" is consistent with orichalcum fabrication at the highest level of magical craft. The key predates the Awakening magically. Its construction required knowledge and access that does not exist in the 6th World outside of the oldest practitioners.

### Astral Properties
The key's aura is difficult to read. Assensing results:

| Successes | Information |
|---|---|
| 1 | Powerful enchantment of a kind never seen before. Aura difficult to read. |
| 2 | Aura of great age — truly ancient, impossible to date. |
| 3 | Magic primarily concerned with unlocking, opening, unbinding. Capable of channeling very powerful forces. |
| 4+ | Power usable for unbinding and other forms of magical unlocking and opening. |

Any magician examining the key astrally experiences a strange tingling sensation during the examination.

### Functional Properties
Functions as a Rating 8 Power Focus for magical feats involving opening, unlocking, or unbinding — specifically including dispelling and banishing. This is an unbonded Power Focus. It does not require bonding by the wielder to function. Any magician who holds it can use it.

The astral resonance of the key matches the Session 01 glyph placed by Aldric — both carry signatures of the same ancient tradition or practitioner lineage.

### Current Status
On Banshee's person per Aldric's explicit instruction. She has been carrying it since Session 12. Aldric's instruction — that the key remain on Banshee at all times — implies individual-level knowledge of the family prior to the meeting. Banshee's Conjuring 14, WIL 14, and Wolf tradition make her the appropriate anchor for an item requiring sustained magical integrity.

### What It Opens
GM controlled entirely. The key opens something at the Tacoma waterfront berth. Aldric knows what it opens. The pier man (Callum Shire) may have contextual knowledge. The Wednesday meeting is where this resolves.

## THE WEAPON FOCI

### Status
Each family member has a bonded weapon focus. The bond was established through formal bonding quests during the prior career. The foci were then subjected to masking quests — metamagic work at the level the family operates at means the masking is extremely effective. The foci are nearly impossible to track in either direction: the family cannot be found through the foci, and the foci cannot be identified as belonging to them through astral examination.

### Sale and Current Possession
The foci were sold during the asset liquidation that Ariel managed during the reconstruction period. The buyers acquired the physical items but have been unable to bond them. The original bond persists. This means the foci are somewhere in the world, in the hands of buyers who cannot use them and likely cannot determine why.

### Rules Implications
The bond never broke. If the family recovers a focus, rebonding is not required. The masking on the bond means even the buyers do not know what they have — they know the items are magical but the full nature of the bond is concealed. This is a live thread when GM chooses to surface it.

## ALDRIC

### Identity
Real name Aldric. Has other names from other times — stated deliberately in Session 12. Human male presenting mid-40s. Grade 4-5 hermetic initiate with decades of practice. Operates outside all current corporate, Lone Star, and standard runner frameworks.

### What He Knows
- The seven family members individually — from months of ritual healing presence during reconstruction
- Each aura in current form — post-genetic-restructuring, not pre-reset identity
- The Laés event and the HMS chemical intervention
- That the memories are potentially recoverable
- The pale architect — knows him personally, withholds deliberately
- Who touched Reyes's chip — withholds deliberately
- The same emotional signature appears around both withheld identities — same entity or different is GM controlled
- Flurry — met her during the reconstruction period, recognized her immediately in Session 12

### What He Does Not Know
- Pre-reset identities of the family
- Their initiate status — he has not demonstrated knowledge of this
- The contents of HMS-7731-CORP beyond what reconstruction involvement would reveal
- Whether the family chose the reset voluntarily or whether the Laés pushed further than intended

### His Relationship to the Key
He placed glyphs that match the key's astral resonance. He built hardware from organometallic materials using fabrication techniques that parallel the key's construction. He writes in proto-Sperethiel older than the current elven tongue. He directed the family to the Tacoma berth and instructed Banshee to carry the key. He knew what the key was and where it needed to go.

### His Fear
He is afraid of what happens if someone else explains things to the family first. This fear implies there is a version of the explanation that would be harmful, incomplete, or manipulative — and that specific other parties are capable of delivering that version. The pale architect is the most likely candidate for the entity he fears will reach the family first.

### Session 12 Confirmed Behaviors
- Allowed Mind Probe without resistance — full emotional transparency, no deception detected
- Flurry recognized him — greeted her in root Sperethiel directly, as an old friend
- Knows Drake — described as "older debts," does not answer to Drake
- Was unsurprised by the Commodore engagement
- Confirmed glyph-placer, confirmed protective watcher
- Delivered Tacoma waterfront address, berth number, "Wednesday," all three archived in 3PR headware

## THE LAÉS — PRIOR CAREER EVENT (TREE ROOTS UNLEASHED)

### Separate Event
The family was dosed with Laés during a prior career run — the Tree Roots Unleashed adventure. This is entirely separate from the reconstruction-period Laés dosing. Two distinct Laés events occurred at different points in the family's career.

### Effect
Standard Laés dosing — retrograde amnesia. This event occurred during active running career, likely before the most recent years. The family may have experienced flashbacks from this event at any point — specific stimuli triggering partial memories that cannot be placed.

### Interaction With the Reset
Two separate memory disruption events layered under the reset creates a complex internal memory architecture. During restoration attempts, a stimulus might trigger a Tree Roots flashback rather than a reset-era memory. The family may confuse which layer a recovered memory belongs to. GM has three distinct memory disruption layers to work with: the prior-career Laés, the reconstruction-period Laés, and the chosen reset.

### Flashback Triggers (Canonical — Tree Roots Source)
- Examining the lapel pin from that run — triggers memory of a banner and a woman giving a speech via simlink projection
- Examining the clothes worn during the run — triggers memory of passing papers to a deferential guard
- Examining the key — triggers memory of a large dining room, a huge meal, a short man bringing a locked box and speaking in hushed tones
- Examining the group's bag of gear — triggers memory of being surrounded by elves, bright lights, loud voices asking the same questions repeatedly

## MEMORY RESTORATION ARCHITECTURE

### The Five Trigger Categories (Canonical)

**Blood/Sacrifice Magic**
Trigger: Encounter blood magic, sacrifice magic, similar items.
Source event: Ivy and Chrome.
Restores: Memory of the item, what it cost.
Power restoration: May restore Infinite Warehouse (loss of magic for 0.5PP spent).

**Insect Spirits — Spider Type**
Trigger: Insect spirits (spider type), Horror-tainted astral space.
Source event: Total Eclipse.
Restores: Combat memory first, context after.
Power restoration: Will restore Traceless Walk (loss of magic for 0.5PP spent).
Note: Abiding hatred wells up before conscious recall — characters act before they know why. Applies to insect spirits generally, not only spider type.

**Great Dragon Contact**
Trigger: Great Dragon contact, Geyswain or Arleesh named, dragon-scale artifacts.
Source event: Dragon Hunt.
Restores: Weight of helping kill a Great Dragon. Arleesh's name feels significant before they know why.
Power restoration: May restore Infinite Warehouse (loss of magic for 0.5PP spent).
Note: Professional recognition layer — "I have done this before" — fired in Session 12 during Drake engagement. Builds with further dragon contact. Geyswain/Arleesh trigger is entirely separate from the general dragon contact trigger.

**Volcano/Great Ghost Dance Sites**
Trigger: Proximity to volcano-affected site or Great Ghost Dance site.
Source event: Harlequin's Back (01/08/57).
Restores: Fragmentary at first, builds with exposure.
Power restoration: Ascending restoration of Lightbringer powers, 2 per event.
Note: Two Harlequin arcs may conflate. The 2050 arc and 2057 Harlequin's Back are distinct events seven years apart. Characters may confuse which memories belong to which event during restoration.

**Physical Visit to Althain (Mt. St. Helens)**
Trigger: Physical visit to Mt. St. Helens.
Restores: Complete restoration, all seven simultaneously.
Power restoration: All listed powers restored.
Note: Do not surface to players. Characters have no conscious reason to go there yet. May be drawn without knowing why — same mechanism as Keystone calling Dag.

### The 4th World Abilities Being Restored

**Lightbringer**
Source: Seeking to destroy Horror in the Salish area.
Restoration: Ascending, 2 per volcano/Great Ghost Dance site event.

**Greater Incarnation**
Source: Blunting the Horror advance.
Function: Unresolved. GM controlled.

**Assumed Path**
Source: 6th World Elven Magic — added path selection on top of Shamanism.

**Infinite Warehouse**
Source: Metaplanar item storage.
Restoration triggers: Blood/sacrifice magic OR Great Dragon contact.

**Traceless Walk**
Restoration trigger: Insect spirits (spider type) or Horror-tainted astral.

### The Three Prior Career Laés Flashback Triggers
These are separate from reset memory restoration — they trigger partial prior-career memories that cannot be placed. They may be confused with reset restoration. See The Laés — Prior Career Event section above for full trigger list.
