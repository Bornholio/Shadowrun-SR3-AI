she is treated as one of the Immortal Elves from the Fourth World and is described as having tried to gain power repeatedly in the Fifth World. Shadowhelix’s Shadowrun entry says she reportedly ruled as Elizabeth I in 1558, was allegedly near Hitler in 1939 according to Aina Dupree, and from 2035 to 2064 secretly sat on the Prince’s Council of Tír Tairngire under the name Sósan Naerain. It also notes that Dunkelzahn’s Will left her the Everliving Flower in 2057

The main Earthdawn books for Alachia, Queen of the Blood Wood are:
The Blood Wood (ED1) — this is the big one. It explicitly says it covers Queen Alachia’s palace, the Elven Court, noble houses, and Blood Wood society.
Elven Nations (ED4) — covers the post-Scourge elven nations and specifically states that Queen Alachia expects the other elven nations to follow Blood Wood’s example.
Mists of Betrayal — an adventure that directly takes the characters into Queen Alachia’s domain in the Blood Wood.

The Neo-Anarchist Streetpedia — has an entry for Alachia.
Shadows of Europe — indexed via Shadowhelix as one of her Shadowrun source references.
Spy Games / Machtspiele – Handbuch für Spione — also indexed as a source connected to her and the Songbirds intelligence angle.
Material connected to Dunkelzahn’s heirs / Will also references her as a beneficiary.
