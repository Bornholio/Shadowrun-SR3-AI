Shorthand and abbreviation table

FA	Free Action
SA	Simple Action
CA	Complex Action
c	Generally means concealment
mp	MegaPulse of memory or storage space required
GP	GigaPulse = 1000mp
port	either a internal cyber data network port (- is consumes, otherwise adds, self linking and brain ports are ignored)
	or TC Port, Tactical computer Sensor port
Generic Port	more specific port that requires a loaded program to interpret configurable devices or alternate methods of linking, for instance via radio or RC Deck

REA or Rea	Reaction, occasionally also R in context shorthand
BOD or Bod	Body, occasionally also B in context shorthand
QUI or Qui	Quickness, occasionally also Q in context shorthand
STR or Str	Strength, occasionally also S in context shorthand
CHA or Cha	Charisma, occasionally also C in context shorthand
INT or Int	Intelligence, occasionally also I in context shorthand
WIL or Wil	Willpower, occasionally also W in context shorthand

Formatting
Spells: Heal-6[],	NAME-FORCE[OPTIONS] [options are things like Fetish, Exclusive]
Skills: Athletics, Bod, 7(13), †Aptitude	NAME, STAT, RATING(AUGMENTED RATING), MODIFIERS Modifiers are things like if skill has edge, allows centering or has martial arts ability possible special rules
Stats: QUI, 11, 11(15), x3 	NAME, Racial MAX, RATING(NATURAL/BIOWARE AUGMENTATION)[CYBER AUGMENTATION], NOTES OR INTRINSIC
